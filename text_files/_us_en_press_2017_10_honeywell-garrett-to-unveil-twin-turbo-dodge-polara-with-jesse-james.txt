Honeywell Garrett To Unveil Twin Turbo Dodge Polara with  Jesse James
October 5, 2018
Honeywell Garrett teams up with Roadster Shop to unveil a Mopar built for one of the most well-known motorcycle builders and fabricators in the business at the world's largest trade show.
TORRANCE, Calif. – Oct. 31, 2017– Honeywell Garrett, a global leader in performance turbochargers, will be unveiling a one-of-a-kind1964 Dodge Polara built for fabricator and bike builder, Jesse James, at theaftermarket's biggest annual trade show, SEMA, in Las Vegas on October 31,2017. This Honeywell Garrett twin-turbocharged, Hemi-powered Polara built by the Roadster Shop features custom Forgeline monoblock wheels, Nitto NT05R tires and sits on a custom Roadster Shop Fast Track chassis. To seethe unveiling live, tune into the Honeywell Garrett Facebook page at 2:00PM on October 31st.
WHERE: Honeywell Garrett Booth # 24865
SEMA Central Hall/Performance Hall
WHEN: Tuesday,October 31st, 2:00pm
For more information on Honeywell Garrett's motorsports efforts and Honeywell Garrett products, visit https://garrett.honeywell.com. To follow the progress of the build and see photos, visit the Roadster Shop website HERE.

Honeywell Aerospace products and services are found on virtually every commercial, defense and space aircraft, and its turbochargers are used by nearly every automaker and truck manufacturer around the world. The Aerospace business unit develops innovative solutions for more fuel-efficient automobiles and airplanes, more direct and on-time flights, safer flying and runway traffic, along with aircraft engines, cockpit and cabin electronics, wireless connectivity services, logistics and more. The business delivers safer, faster, and more efficient and comfortable transportation-related experiences worldwide. For more information, visitwww.honeywell.com/us/en.
