Honeywell statement on US Mexico Canada agreement
December 5, 2018
MORRIS PLAINS, December 5, 2018 – Honeywell (NYSE: HON) has issued the following statement in support of the U.S.-Mexico-Canada Agreement.
“Honeywell welcomes the recent signing of the U.S.-Mexico-Canada Agreement. This landmark agreement takes important steps to modernize the trade rules for the region, including enabling the digital economy. We urge the three countries to move expeditiously to approve the agreement to ensure continued economic growth and stability in the region.”
Honeywell (www.honeywell.com/us/en) is a Fortune 100 technology company that delivers industry specific solutions that include aerospace products and services; control technologies for buildings and industry; and performance materials globally. Our technologies help everything from aircraft, buildings, manufacturing plants, supply chains, and workers become more connected to make our world smarter, safer, and more sustainable. For more news and information on Honeywell, please visit www.honeywell.com/us/en/news.
