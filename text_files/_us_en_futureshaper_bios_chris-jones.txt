Chris Jones
Senior Director, Business Development
Chris is humanizing the digital disruption.
Chris started his career at Honeywell 26 years ago as a chemical and plant operator. Today, he’s helping people have a safer, more reliable, and more sustainable experience in industrial spaces through autonomous technology. In industries where 30% of accidents occur due to human error, Chris’ team is developing new solutions that move the brunt of the work on the plant site to digital technologies so employees can focus on safer, more value-added activities.
