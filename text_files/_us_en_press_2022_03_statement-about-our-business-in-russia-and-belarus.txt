Statement About Our Business in Russia and Belarus
August 16, 2022
In response to the Russian invasion of Ukraine, we have decided to wind down our existing businesses and operations in Russia. Our number one priority is the safety and security of our employees and partners in the region. Our collective thoughts are with the millions of people affected by the conflict.
Bevin Maguire
