Pranav Kumbhar
IP Analyst II
Pranav is combining his legal and technical expertise to transform the environment.
Pranav believes we should all live by example. Whether it’s gifting family and friends sustainable items or being intentional on his use of water and electricity, Pranav is making personal changes every day to impact the environment for the better. At Honeywell, Pranav plays a critical role in bringing our sustainability portfolio to the market so that other companies and people can utilize them now to shape the future climate change agenda.
