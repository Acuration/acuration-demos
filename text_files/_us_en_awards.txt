National Partner of the Year, Golden Torch Awards, March 2022
At the 48th National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) Convention, Honeywell was recognized during the Golden Torch Awards ceremony as the National Partner of the Year for its NSBE Integrated Pipeline Program.
