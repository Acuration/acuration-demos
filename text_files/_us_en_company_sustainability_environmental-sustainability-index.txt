Gauging Sentiment Around The Pace And Adoption Of Climate Tech
Produced in collaboration with The Futurum Group, the index compiles insights from more than 750 business leaders directly involved with their organization’s Environmental Sustainability (ES) initiatives. The Environmental Sustainability Index (ESI) is produced quarterly to track global shifts in corporate sentiment and sustainability investment. This is the fifth edition since the report’s inception in Q4’22, enabling year-over-year comparisons for the first time.

About The Index
The Environmental Sustainability Index is produced quarterly by Honeywell in collaboration with Futurum Research. The goal of the index is to inform the global community on the current and anticipated adoption of technologies that directly support Environmental and Sustainability initiatives.
