PROGRESS SO FAR
April 2021:
We committed to carbon neutrality with respect to Scopes 1 and 2 greenhouse gases, and to further quantify and address our Scope 3 emissions.
February 2022:
We submitted our Commitment Letter to the Science Based Targets initiative (SBTi) committing to develop a science-based target in line with their protocols that will include Scope 3 emissions.
We joined the US Department of Energy’s Better Climate Challenge, committing to reduce our US Scopes 1 and 2 greenhouse gases by 50% from a 2018 baseline by no later than 2030.
