Ye Wang
Advanced User Experience Designer
Ye leverages design software tools to change the way people think and create.
All aspects of our work and personal lives are influenced by technology. For Ye, it’s more important than ever to use software technologies to help reduce the emission of greenhouse gases. From elevating production efficiency to improving workforce safety, Ye is a groundbreaker in building software that’s being used in some of the largest factories in the world. A creator at heart, Ye’s mission is to break the information gap and design sustainable solutions for people to improve their daily lives.
