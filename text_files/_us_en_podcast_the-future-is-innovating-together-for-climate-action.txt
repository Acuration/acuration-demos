The Future Is... Innovating Together For Climate Action
Honeywell President and COO Vimal Kapur joins this podcast episode to discuss the WEF Annual Meeting, technologies enabling the global energy transition, and the role of communities in climate action.
Innovation will unlock solutions to the world's most pressing challenges.
Teamwork will help us get there faster.
Honeywell's President and Chief Operating Officer Vimal Kapur shares his views on the 2023 World Economic Forum Annual Meeting and the level of public and private co-innovation needed to make meaningful global progress toward mitigating the impacts of climate change.
From advancements in digitalization designed to help industrial companies measure their emissions, to technologies enabling new forms of transportation that will shape your commute in the future — this podcast episode gives a glimpse into breakthrough technologies that are available today or coming soon.
Watch this episode on YouTube, and listen on Apple, Spotify, iHeart, Google, Stitcher or TuneIn.
Learn more about the progress Honeywell is making toward our sustainability goals.
