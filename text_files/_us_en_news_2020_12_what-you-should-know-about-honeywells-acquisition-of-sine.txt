What You Should Know About Honeywell's Acquisition of Sine
Acquisition of software as a service (SaaS) company Sine Group will enhance Honeywell Forge’s return-to-work capabilities, mobile device experience and connected buildings solutions.
Managing workplaces and workforces has gone from clipboards to smart phones. Our acquisition of Sine will help enhance the possibilities on a global scale.
Here are the details.
What: Sine is a mobile workforce management solution.
Headquarters: Adelaide, Australia
Founded: 2014
What Sine does: The Software-as-a-Service (SAAS) handles visitor management, workplace and supply chain solutions.
Industries covered: Schools, offices, retail, industrial and mining facilities use functionality like check-in for visitors, contractors, staff, students and couriers.
How it fits in the Honeywell portfolio: Sine’s expertise will help to enhance Honeywell Forge, an enterprise performance management (EPM) offering, with mobile solutions that help organizations to make their operations safer and more productive.
Why it’s so innovative: The new capabilities extend Honeywell Forge’s native edge-to-cloud, data-driven architecture with user-friendly mobile capabilities designed to digitally transform industrial operations.
Return-to-workplace: Sine has features to enable organizations to manage their Covid-19 protocols, including pre-screening, thermal camera integrations, capacity management and contact tracing.
