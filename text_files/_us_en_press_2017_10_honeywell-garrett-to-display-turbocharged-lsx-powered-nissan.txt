Honeywell Garrett to display Turbocharged LSX-Powered Nissan
October 5, 2018
Steve “Baggsy” Biagioni’s 1,200 horsepowerV8 GT-R drift car will appear at the SEMA Show for the first time.
TORRANCE, Calif. – Oct. 31, 2017 - The world’s only turbocharged LSX-powered Nissan GT-R will be on display at the Honeywell Garrett booth at the 2017 SEMA Show in Las Vegas on October 31, 2017. This vehicle owned by British Drift Champion Steve “Baggsy” Biagioni, was built from the ground up along with fabrication and fine tuning by SB Motorsports. It produces 1200 horsepower and 1150 lb-ft of torque from its Honeywell Garrett GTX4718R-turbocharged LSX 454ciV8. This drift-spec GT-R also features a new Liberty Walk V1 body kit and Rotiform LHR wheels. “Baggsy” will be available for interviews and will be at the Honeywell Garrett booth throughout the SEMA Show.
WHERE: Honeywell Garrett Booth # 24865
SEMA Central Hall/ Performance Hall
WHEN: Tuesday, October 31st to Friday, November 3rd
For more information on Honeywell Garrett’s motorsports efforts and Honeywell Garrett products, visit https://www.garrett.honeywell.com.
