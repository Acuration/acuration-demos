Meet a #futureshaper: Rabindranath Raul
His dream is to update software in planes - on the fly.
Rabindranath Raul has a passion for automation.
For 23 years, Honeywell’s open culture and flexibility has motivated him to go above and beyond.
Based in Bangalore, India; here is his #futureshaper story.
When you were a child, what did you want to be when you grew up?
Medical doctor.
What brought you to Honeywell?
I was attracted to Honeywell’s technologies and vision in making the world a safer place and the chance to make human beings more efficient and productive through unique solutions and services.
What gets you excited about going to work every day?
Honeywell works on diversified technologies, products, platforms which helped me to learn new things every day.
What do you do?
We make the systems which enable cars, trains, and aircraft to run or fly efficiently without any pilot/driver or operator.
If you were a fortune reader, what would you predict as the future of your industry?
Integrated diversified autonomous systems augmented with evolving artificial intelligence/machine learning will end up doing most of the work and resulting improved efficiency of the overall ecosystem by multifold.
In your eyes, what qualities does a #futureshaper possess?
A #futureshaper goes beyond their imagination, thinks big and commits and has the courage to make an idea happen.
What advice would you give someone who wants to follow in your footsteps?
Whatever it is, if you sign up for it, do it heartily. To me, Honeywell is a great place where you can challenge yourself and achieve your aspirations.
When you are not working, what do you do for fun?
Passing time with my kids and networking with my friends.
