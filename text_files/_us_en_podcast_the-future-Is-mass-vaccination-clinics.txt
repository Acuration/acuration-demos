The Future Is… Mass Vaccination Clinics
Tom Glick, President of Tepper Sports and Entertainment, and Dr. Scott Rissmiller, executive vice president and chief physician executive of Atrium Health, go behind the scenes of mass vaccination clinics.
The future of vaccinating thousands of people at a time will be mass vaccination clinics.
Those events have been held successfully at the stadium where the Carolina Panthers play in Charlotte.
Tom Glick, President of Tepper Sports and Entertainment, and Dr. Scott Rissmiller, executive vice president and chief physician executive of Atrium Health talk about the teamwork that make those clinics successful.
“It was smooth and efficient for people,” Tom said.
