Sampada Bokade
Intellectual Property Supervisor
Sampada is shifting today’s technologies to more sustainable solutions.
Intellectual property in sustainable energy and oil and gas industries is changing rapidly, thanks in part to Sampada. Each day, she and her team are tackling possible IP roadblocks and identifying potential solutions, which can ultimately lead to new technologies and products. She has a fostered advancements in sustainable technologies including Carbon Capture and Ecofining™ renewable fuel. Whether it’s a small or large initiative, Sampada believes in celebrating every accomplishment. To her, every experience is an opportunity to become your best self and do better for others.
