The Future Is... Cyber Secure
Cybersecurity expert Sema Tutucu explains how businesses can protect against hackers.
The technology that businesses depend on can be vulnerable to hackers. Those bad actors can threaten companies' uptime and bottom line.
Cybersecurity expert Sema Tutucu explains how the industrial world is at risk and why they should protect themselves.
Sema breaks down the difference between "black hats" a common term in the cyber world that means the bad guys of hackers.
"They don't have good intentions and they also most of the time do harm," Sema said.
So what do we do about them?
Find out on The Future Is... podcast on your favorite platform.
