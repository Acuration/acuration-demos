Honeywell Issues Statement on Phase One U.S. China Trade Deal
December 13, 2019
CHARLOTTE, N.C., December 13, 2019 -- Honeywell (NYSE: HON) today issued the following statement in response to the Phase One U.S.-China trade deal announced this week:
“Honeywell congratulates President Trump and President Xi for concluding a Phase One trade deal that sets the stage for further economic cooperation between the world’s two largest economies. While more work is needed to address open issues, the deal is a very positive and welcome step toward promoting trade between the two countries.”
Honeywell (www.honeywell.com/us/en) is a Fortune 100 technology company that delivers industry-specific solutions that include aerospace products and services; control technologies for buildings and industry; and performance materials globally. Our technologies help aircraft, buildings, manufacturing plants, supply chains, and workers become more connected to make our world smarter, safer, and more sustainable. For more news and information on Honeywell, please visit www.honeywell.com/us/en/news.
