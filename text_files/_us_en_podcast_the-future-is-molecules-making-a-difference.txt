The Future Is... Molecules Making a Difference
Chief technology officer Maribel Wilson talks about how small molecules have a big impact.
Shaving cream, hairspray and keyboard cleaners all use aerosols that have historically been harmful to the planet.
But there is an alternative that has a low Global Warming Potential (GWP) and can also be used in propellants like aerosols.
Host Joe Toubes interviews Maribel about how these molecules work.
And the impact is huge.
"If you look at the CO2 equivalent that we’ve pulled off the market, just this year alone, it’s the equivalent of taking 33 million cars off the road,” she says.
