The Future Is... Innovation in Healthcare
Robert Robinson, vice president and general manager of Honeywell Healthcare Solutions, shares how advancements in sensing technology are expected to help hospitals better prepare for potential future pandemics and address the needs of an aging population.
In the coming years, you can expect technology to have a larger presence in your personal healthcare.
Why? To enable earlier intervention of health complications, give you and your care provider access to real-time data, and help health professionals deliver top-notch care.
Robert Robinson, vice president and general manager of Honeywell Healthcare Solutions, joins the podcast to discuss how advancements in sensing technology will enable hospitals and healthcare systems to better respond to future public health crises and care for an aging population that is increasing in size.
