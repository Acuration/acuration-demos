The Future Is… Seamless Supply Chains
Chief Supply Chain Officer Torsten Pilz is driven by solving complex engineering and supply chain problems.
During the pandemic, supply chains around the world faced scrutiny as shortages for cleaning supplies and even toilet paper made headlines.
Meanwhile, Honeywell acted to predict challenges from the pandemic and heed them off.
“We really tried to anticipate things early on and we reacted quickly,” said Chief Supply Chain Officer Torsten Pilz.
How did that go and what does the supply chain of the future look like?
