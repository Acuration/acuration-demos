The Future Is… Innovative Supply Chains
Honeywell Chief Supply Chain Officer Torsten Pilz, Udo Lange of Federal Express Logistics and Nitin Chaudhary from Fresh Direct talk about the future of supply chains.
Healthy supply chains are vital to modern life, from ecommerce to retail.
Torsten, Udo and Nitin discuss how businesses can protect and innovate their supply chains.
Companies must invest in technology, especially advanced analytics and decision support systems to improve supply forecast accuracy, Nitin said.
