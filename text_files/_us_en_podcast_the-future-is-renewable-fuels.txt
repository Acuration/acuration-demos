The Future Is... Renewable Fuels
Leigh Abrams, offering manager for Honeywell's renewable fuels division, breaks down the technologies enabling sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) production, and ultimately, a cleaner air travel industry.
Leigh Abrams, offering manager for Honeywell's renewable fuels division, joins Honeywell's Chief Marketing Officer Laura Kelleher to discuss the latest innovations in sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) production.
Learn how ethanol – which you likely know from filling your car at the pump – is expected to play a role in powering more planes with low-carbon jet fuel in the future.
