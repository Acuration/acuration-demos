Taylor Alberstadt
Senior Director, Sales & Marketing
Taylor is taking transportation to new heights.
Remember The Jetsons? The cartoon sitcom where the family’s mode of transportation was a flying car? Taylor is helping make this a reality. He’s leading the charge in creating a new market in advanced air mobility, where electric aircraft move people and goods faster, cheaper and with a fraction of their current energy consumption and emissions. His ultimate goal? Enabling a more efficient and sustainable world today and for future generations.
