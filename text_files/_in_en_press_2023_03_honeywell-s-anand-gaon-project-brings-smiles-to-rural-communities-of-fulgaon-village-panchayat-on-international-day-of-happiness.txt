Honeywell's ‘Anand Gaon’ Project Brings Smiles To Rural Communities Of Fulgaon Village Panchayat On International Day Of Happiness
The Initiative aims to enhance the lives of rural communities of Fulgaon Village Panchayat.
20 March 2023
India, March 20, 2023: Honeywell Hometown Solutions India Foundation (HHSIF), the philanthropic arm of Honeywell (NASDAQ: HON), has successfully completed the redevelopment of the first village, Fulgaon, under its project ‘Anand Gaon’. This project is an initiative by Honeywell to improve the quality of life for the rural communities in the vicinity of Honeywell entities by providing infrastructure support and basic amenities. Fulgaon village Panchayat of Maharashtra was adopted in the year FY 2022-23 as the first village under the project ‘Anand Gaon’.
Today, on the day of UN’s ‘International Day of Happiness’ Honeywell conducted the handover ceremony of “Anand Gaon’. This is under the Holistic and Sustainable Community Development thematic focus of HHSIF.
The HHSIF partnered with Americares India Foundation (AIF) and the staff of HHSIF and AIF, with the support of local leadership at HAIL plant in Fulgaon conducted a need assessment and interacted with the Panchayat members to understand the requirement. Based on the request and assessment study, Honeywell earmarked resources to upgrade the school and provided solar streetlights to the village, and solar panels to the panchayat office and make the school energy efficient.
The upgraded amenities will put a smile on the face of 300+ rural children and will impact the lives of 10,000+ population. The refurbished school and other facilities were unveiled and handed over by Mr. Ashish Gaikwad, managing director, Honeywell Automation India Limited on behalf of Honeywell, to Mrs. Mandakini Sakore, president, Gram Panchayat, Fulgaon.
“We are proud to have partnered with Americares India Foundation and the local leadership at HAIL plant in Fulgaon to complete the project 'Anand Gaon',” said Ashish Gaikwad, managing director, Honeywell Automation India. He also added, “Through this project, we have upgraded the school, provided solar streetlights to the village, and solar panels to the Panchayat office to make it energy-efficient, which will have a positive impact on the lives of 10,000+ people in the community. It is our privilege to hand over these upgraded facilities on the International Day of Happiness. We believe that businesses have a responsibility to support the communities they operate in, especially those living in rural areas. Honeywell remains dedicated to creating a sustainable and inclusive future, and the 'Anand Gaon' initiative is a testament to this commitment.”
‘Anand Gaon’ is a significant milestone for Honeywell and its commitment to improving the quality of life in the communities where it operates.
Meenakshi Batra, country director, Americares India Foundation, said, “Our partnership with Honeywell will help the redevelopment of the first village, Fulgaon, under its project ‘Anand Gaon’. With this collaboration, we aim to make a difference by Holistic and Sustainable Community Development improving the quality of life in communities”
About Honeywell Hometown Solution India Foundation:
Honeywell Hometown Solutions India Foundation (HHSIF) is a not-for-profit organization that deploys Honeywell’s corporate social responsibility (CSR) efforts in five critical areas: education, skill and research, diversity and inclusion, natural resources and environment, holistic and sustainable community development, and  humanitarian relief. HHSIF’s programs include Honeywell STEM education for young girls,  upskilling program for youth to build a future-ready workforce; Honeywell model village for holistic rural development; Honeywell start-up support program  that incubates startups in deep science and deep technology segments,  Honeywell global center for affordable and clean energy and a ten year long tree plantation drive across the country under the banner of ‘Plant the Future’. About Americares India Foundation:
In 2006, Americares India Foundation was registered as a public charitable trust in India. With a mission to help communities prepare for, respond to, and recover from disasters, increase access to critical medicine and medical supplies, improve and expand clinical services, prevent disease, and promote good health in vulnerable communities. Americares India Foundation accomplishes its mission to help more people live longer healthier lives through five core programmatic initiatives targeting health and holistic development for the underprivileged.
About Honeywell:
Honeywell (www.honeywell.com) delivers industry-specific solutions that include aerospace products and services; control technologies for buildings and industry; and performance materials globally. Our technologies help aircraft, buildings, manufacturing plants, supply chains, and workers become more connected to make our world smarter, safer, and more sustainable.
