What You Should Know About Honeywell’s Acquisition of Sparta Systems
The AI-enabled Software as a Service (SaaS) company will enhance our life sciences and software capabilities.
Bringing new medical therapies to market requires precision, quality assurance and process verification. By acquiring Sparta Systems, we will help life sciences companies innovate.
Here are the details:
What: Sparta Systems provides enterprise quality management software (QMS), including a next-generation SaaS platform, for the life sciences industry.
Headquarters: Hamilton, N.J.
Founded: 1994
What Sparta Systems does: Pharmaceutical, biopharma, medical device and other life sciences customers use the software for digital quality management to automate and standardize quality processes.
Sparta’s reach: More than 400 customers, including 42 of the top 50 pharma companies and 33 of the top 50 medical device companies.
How it fits in the Honeywell portfolio: We have provided the world’s leading drug manufacturers and biomedical firms with advanced automation and process control technologies for over 30 years. Sparta Systems will add leadership in next-generation, cloud and digital QMS SaaS platforms, analytics and artificial intelligence with augmented human decision-making capabilities.
The plan: Sparta will combine with our enterprise performance solution Honeywell Forge and Experion Process Knowledge System to enhance value for life sciences customers in the U.S., Europe and Asia.
Why it’s so innovative: The life science industry will benefit from solutions that improve quality assurance, reduce compliance costs, verify process performance and help identify quality deviations in advance of the manufacturing process.
