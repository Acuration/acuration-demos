Our operating system
Making Business Happen
Innovation is at the core of what we do. To achieve this, Honeywell Accelerator provides an approach to deliver every time and provide the best customer experience.
Honeywell Accelerator brings together our powerful frameworks and toolkits – supported by a robust learning program – to substantially improve end-to-end processes, digital transformation and business outcomes. It is a common language to understand how we run our business – one centralized resource providing access to the way we do things.
The operating system makes accessible the tools, processes and best practices to enable Honeywell to operate smarter and faster to solve the toughest customer challenges and quality demands.
By promoting career development and expanding employee knowledge, Honeywell is forwarding initiatives critical to long-term success such as connected software, digitization, supply chain transformation, building exceptional talent and customer-centricity.
Honeywell Accelerator helps us more quickly and efficiently enable a safer, more comfortable and more productive world – no matter what challenges the world throws at us.
