Honeywell Named In 2021 Gartner Magic Quadrant For Managed Mobile Services, Global Report
Honeywell Enterprise Mobility recognized as a Challenger based on its Ability to Execute and Completeness of Vision
May 11, 2021
CHARLOTTE, N.C., May 11, 2021 -- Honeywell (Nasdaq: HON) today announced it was recognized by leading research and advisory firm Gartner in the 2021 Magic Quadrant for Managed Mobile Services, Global1 report. Gartner’s Magic Quadrants evaluates vendors on their ability to execute and completeness of vision.
Honeywell Enterprise Mobility is one of two managed mobile services vendors named as a Challenger in this report.
“The COVID-19 pandemic has shifted technology priorities for businesses across the globe to ensure mobile connectivity and IT support is available whenever needed,” said Kevin Dehoff, president of Honeywell’s Productivity Solutions and Services business. “We believe the recognition by Gartner as a Challenger in this space underscores the value and importance that managed mobile solutions from Honeywell Enterprise Mobility provide to keep mobile workers and supply chains moving.”
Honeywell’s managed mobile services support national, multinational and global enterprises with a single solution for managing companies’ entire mobile environments of corporate-liable and individual-liable devices that employees use to access company resources and information. Honeywell partners with leading voice and data businesses and telecom expense companies to support nearly two million devices.
The solution is designed to help customers get the managed mobile services offering they need, which includes sourcing and logistics support, managed unified endpoint management services, security management, asset and financial management and program management.
For more than a decade, Honeywell Enterprise Mobility has been supporting the lifecycle management of mobile devices, giving companies of all sizes around the world the confidence of having leading-class technologies combined with service and support to keep them operational for longer periods of time.
Of the 15 providers rated in the 2021 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Managed Mobile Services, Honeywell is the only vendor that is also an Android Enterprise Recommended provider, a Google-led initiative designed to give customers more confidence in deploying Android solutions to make sure their enterprise mobility deployment is consistent and current.
Honeywell has been named for the 8th time in Gartner’s Magic Quadrant for Managed Mobile Services*. For more information about Honeywell’s Managed Mobility services, visit https://sps.honeywell.com/us/en/services/productivity/managed-services.

Honeywell Safety and Productivity Solutions (SPS) provides products, software and connected solutions that improve productivity, workplace safety and asset performance for our customers across the globe. We deliver on this promise through industry-leading mobile devices, software, cloud technology and automation solutions, the broadest range of personal protective equipment and gas detection technology, and custom-engineered sensors, switches and controls. For more information, please visit: sps.honeywell.com.
Honeywell (https://www.honeywell.com/us/en) is a Fortune 100 technology company that delivers industry-specific solutions that include aerospace products and services; control technologies for buildings and industry; and performance materials globally. Our technologies help aircraft, buildings, manufacturing plants, supply chains, and workers become more connected to make our world smarter, safer, and more sustainable. For more news and information on Honeywell, please visit www.honeywell.com/us/en/news.
