senior vice president global government relations february 28 2017 morris plains nj feb 28 2017 jim carroll has been named senior vice president global government relations he will report to kate adams senior vice president and general counsel effective march 31 jim brings more than 30 years of experience and extensive knowledge of high growth regions and technology to honeywell said adams government relations is an important growth driver for honeywell especially in this fastchanging environment it is more important than ever for the company to educate policymakers and key decision makers around the world about who we are and the tremendous benefits we can offer through our leading technology portfolio jim will be responsible for leading all global federal and state government relations activities and developing and executing honeywells strategic global corporate regulatory and government relations strategies he also will be responsible for ensuring strong relationships with regulators and government leaders prior to joining honeywell jim served as the global managing director of government relations for accenture endash a global professional services company of 394000 employees endash where he was responsible for managing a globally diverse team of professionals across the us europe and asia he also led the companys us corporate citizenship program previously jim worked as vice president for the livingston group and as the vice president congressional relations for comsat corporation he started his career in a number of staff roles for us senator warren b rudman and as an executive director of the new hampshire dole for president committee jim serves on a number of professional and charitable boards including the us chamber of commerce the information technology industry council and junior achievement usa jim earned a bachelors degree in history from the university of new hampshire in durham new hampshire honeywell
