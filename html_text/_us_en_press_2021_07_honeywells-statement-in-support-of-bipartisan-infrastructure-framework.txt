bipartisan infrastructure framework july 15 2021 honeywell issued the following statement today in support of the bipartisan infrastructure framework honeywell supports the bipartisan efforts to make significant investments in our nations infrastructure including roads bridges and energy modernization to improve us competitiveness and foster safety and prosperity for countless americans we are encouraged by the hard work on both sides of the aisle to find compromise on this important issue and are hopeful it can be a foundation for continued bipartisan action in support of longterm economic
