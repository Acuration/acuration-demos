morris plains nj feb 20 2018 prnewswire honeywell nyse hon has announced today the appointment of robert vislosky as chief procurement officer with responsibilities for the execution of the companys global procurement and sourcing strategy he succeeds mike slomke who has retired after 23 years of honeywell service roberts extensive expertise and demonstrated track record in leading procurement supply chain and global sourcing excellence will add immediate value to our company said mark james senior vice president human resources procurement and communications we expect robert to continue to consolidate honeywells global spend to drive margin improvement vislosky joins honeywell following chief procurement officer roles with wellknown global companies including xpo logistics valspar and reynolds group as well as seniorlevel procurement supply chain and operations roles with alcoa hb fuller and nova chemicals he earned bachelors and masters degrees in chemical engineering from the university of pittsburgh he is a certified six sigma black belt and serves on the board of advisors for the carlson school of supply chain and operations at the university of minnesota honeywell
