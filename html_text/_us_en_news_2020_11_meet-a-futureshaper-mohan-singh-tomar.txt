you excited about going to work every day the variety of things i work on each day and each week and the limitless learning and freedom to explore new ideas it is even more dynamic in my current role as an innovation leader where i get to debate and nurture new ideas daily if you were a fortune reader what would you predict as the future of your industry we are witnessing an unprecedented transformation in each industry due to rapid adoption of connected and artificial intelligence advancements in computing capabilities will leapfrog with quantum computers i believe industry boundaries will start to blur in your eyes what qualities does a futureshaper possess a futureshaper is curious knows the customer market and technology creative applies technology to develop world class products and solve problems contrarian has perspectives which other dont tenacious does not give up easily but is not stubborn and agile adopts to change and learns quickly what advice would you give someone who wants to follow in your footsteps enjoy your time at work interact with people learn from them and help others understand the big picture do not get bogged down in technology alone be the best in whatever you do when you are not working what do you do for fun i take long evening walks with my wife to catch up and spend time with my boys and our dog i also love to spend time reading
