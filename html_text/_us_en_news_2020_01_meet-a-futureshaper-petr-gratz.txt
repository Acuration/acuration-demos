an engineer or researcher what brought you to honeywell a friend of mine mentioned honeywell was opening a new design center and asked if i would join him for their presentation i ended up joining him and i am glad i did because this meeting led to a job offer i ended up starting a few months later ive now been here more than 16 years what gets you excited about going to work every day seeing my team grow i love developing the people and talent on my teams innovations every day i get the opportunity to work on something cool and new did you ever imagine you would end up in the job you are now not really i thought i would stay with research when i was doing my phd it wasnt until later i realized that i really enjoyed working with people developing them and making them successful if you were
