partnership and growth with thailand march 15 2018 bangkok thailand march 13 2018 honeywell nyse hon today reiterated its power of connecteddriven investment and business expansion plans in thailand committed to strengthening its partnership with thailand business industry and government as a premier softwareindustrial company honeywell is well positioned to contribute to the governments thailand 40 vision which focuses on transforming thailand into a valuebased and innovationdriven digital economy honeywells strong industrial heritage gives it the unique ability to blend physical products with software to make its solutions more connected efficient and productive about half of honeywells 23000 engineers globally are software engineers and the company expects approximately 60 percent of its growth over the next five years will be connected to software thailand is a key strategic market among our high growth regions which benefits from having ambitious industrial sectors that embrace innovation we expect our growth rate in this market to be well above thailands gdp growth over the next five years said shane tedjarati president of honeywell global high growth regions whether our customers are running petrochemical plants aircraft fleets or skyscrapers their operations are generating vast quantities of data that has untapped customer value locked within it our technologies help customers extract that value and in doing so enable them to make better informed decisions that improve the safety security efficiency and productivity of their operations for more than two decades honeywell has grown along with thailand industry enhancing its infrastructure network and supporting its economic development honeywell maintains a strong leadership position in providing highquality home and building technologies safety and productivity solutions performance material technologies and aerospace products and systems to local customers as thailand continues to develop a smarter safer and more sustainable society we believe thailand offers tremendous growth potential due to its favorable demographics and alignment with honeywells portfolio and global macro trends such as the significant rise in middle class rapid urbanization growing demand for green and efficient energy safety and security and digitization recent trade data has been largely positive and per the national economic and social development board nesdb thailands gdp grew by 4 in the last quarter of 2017 and should keep rising this year said mai trang thanh president of honeywell indochina we are very excited to be here and we are fully committed to contributing towards advancing thailands economic and social growth most recently honeywell has been contributing to thailands macro development progress with innovative products and technologies tesco lotus a leading supermarket retailer in thailand and part of tesco group began converting 900 tesco lotus express stores to honeywells solstice n40 refrigerant to voluntarily reduce carbon emissions by converting all of its 1500 express stores in thailand over the next two years to honeywells solstice n40 r448a the retail giant aims to achieve energy savings of up to 10 percent equal to removing more than 67000 cars from the road honeywell supplies auxiliary power units apus mechanical and avionics wheel brake products and aftermarket services including retrofits mods and upgrades on all aircraft fleet types to thai airways bangkok airways nokscoot nokair and other airlines and mro based in thailand honeywell helps large real estate owners in thailand develop integrated building solutions to enhance their operational efficiencies and improve their tenant experience honeywell also works on some key government infrastructure projects by providing innovative building technology solutions to make it right and fast honeywell wwwhoneywellcomusen is a fortune 100 softwareindustrial company that delivers industry
