leadership webcast series featuring vimal kapur and greg lewis november 8 2023 charlotte nc nov 8 2023 prnewswire honeywell nasdaq hon today announced that the next installment of its live leadership webcast series for investors will feature a roundtable discussion with vimal kapur chief executive officer of honeywell and greg lewis senior vice president and chief financial officer the discussion will be moderated by nigel coe managing director and senior analyst electrical equipment and multiindustry at wolfe research and will take place on friday december 1 2023 from 1000 am 1045 am est
