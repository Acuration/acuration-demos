emergency response teams save lives honeywell enhances search and rescue and assettracking software solutions to help emergency response teams save lives charlotte nc october 13 2020 honeywell nyse hon today announced new features to its portfolio of search and rescue software solutions that help countries and emergency response organizations perform rescue operations and track and locate important assets such as a downed plane or a missing ship honeywell global tracking hgt has more than 35 years of experience delivering endtoend search and rescue sar and highvalue asset tracking solutions including satellite ground stations mission control centers mccs rescue coordination centers rccs satellite modems and airtime solutions from combining lower earth orbit leo and geostationary earth orbit geo ground station processing to creating a middle earth orbit search and rescue meosar ground station network honeywell has solutions that improve beacon detections and location accuracy this year hgt customers can benefit from following software solutions network location processor nlp600 10 is a next generation network location processor that leverages the use of the cospas sarsat network it is the cloud enabled solution in compliance with latest cyber security and data privacy requirements it features a webbased gui application with advanced gis that is accessible by mcc and rcc operators nlp600 10 advantages over standalone meolut ground station include widening the coverage area improving beacons detections probability and location accuracy at the edge of coverage areas eliminating the gaps between multiple ground stations eliminating system anomalies improving slow and fastmoving beacon detections and localization rcc software sarmaster 600 14 is an advanced sar incident management system that integrates multiple online and local data sources such as mapping weather incident information sar resources ship and aircraft registries all in a single control center view its flexible architecture supports rapid integration with external systems the webbased application which can be hosted securely at the customer data center or on the honeywell managed cloud requires no special software on the users workstation it provides rccs around the world with complete situational awareness in an integrated view honeywell meosar digital beam former dbf antenna meolut 30 for lband satellites the new smart antenna is costeffective with full remote control monitoring capabilities and dual polarization tracks galileo glonass gps beidou bds with simultaneous lhcp and rhcp reception with superior meolut performance that exceeds cospassarsat specifications and the capability of tracking low elevation satellites with advanced interference cancellation design the smart antenna improves beacon detection localization accuracy and widens the coverage area full redundant system that is easy to operate and maintain with zero down time during system upgrade and maintenance the gmpt401 personal tracker is an integrated costeffective portable satellite device that leverages global iridium satellites for tracking monitoring and communicating with personnel in remote locations rugged and lightweight with hazardous area certification it provides location tracking and panic button capability when used alone when paired with an android or ios smartphone it becomes a comprehensive messaging platform when standard means of communications arent available and is a powerful tool for helping organizations manage their corporate duty of care requirements with an interactive sos feature users will know that their panic button activation has been received hgt delivers reliable and affordable solutions to provide companies with greater visibility and operational control and helps governments and emergencyresponse teams save lives these connected solutions are easy to install maintain and are satellitebased with worldwide coverage in addition they are integrated and supported by backend infrastructure satellite networking and signal processing equipment and incident management software supporting dozens of organizations worldwide honeywell global tracking continues to be a leader in the deployment of systems helping the cospassarsat network to save hundreds of lives around the globe each year about honeywell hgt is a global leader in asset tracking and monitoring solutions understanding the unique challenges involved in tracking remote assets hgt provides fully integrated search and rescue sar solutions based on the international cospassarsat standards the companys sar products and solutions have been at the center of search and rescue operations around the globe for decades currently the solutions are used by organizations in 33 countries to help save lives by receiving and processing distress data from cospassarsat satellites allowing organizations to pinpoint emergency locations honeywell
