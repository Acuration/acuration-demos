hispanic heritage month get to know a futureshaper who helps cultivate community and comradery every day tightknit thats how kevin valenzuela a materials handler describes the bonds hes formed at a honeywell distribution center in el paso texas where he is part of a team that helps manage materials used to develop solutions for our building technologies business kevin a member of our hispanic employee network is passionate about bringing people together which is why he plays a big role in organizing activities to commemorate holidays and recognize employees celebrating career anniversaries at the el paso facility which has a rich hispanic heritage its important to me that i truly get to know the people i work with kevin said we asked this futureshaper a few questions to get to know how he celebrates hispanic heritage and why community is important at work what are some events that made this hispanic heritage month meaningful for you as a facility we hosted a food drive that supported food banks in el paso so we could give back to the community as a team we also hosted a mexican independence day celebration where employees enjoyed traditional foods and relaxed with one another what is special about celebrating hispanic heritage with your colleagues the thing most exciting to me is getting to know different people and feeling connected with one another at our site alone we have people of many different cultures so i feel lucky to get to know their traditions and bond over shared experiences how do you think the events you help organize help foster a unique community distribution centers are large but here i feel that we are all bonded in some way i can tell that from spending time together talking about our families and cultures its helped us communicate better and foster trust on the job kevin added the community here means a lot to me because this is my second family its a great feeling to be excited to come to work learn more about how
