alicia kempf is like a pastry chef in the same way a baker would use kitchen tools and follow a specific recipe to make cookies she makes sure every manufacturing batch is perfect whether thats making batches of pharmaceuticals food beverages or making specialty chemicals she guarantees consistency and quality based in fort washington pennsylvania here is her futureshaper story when you were a child what did you want to be when you grew up when i was a child i wanted to be an inventor i loved the idea of coming up with a new idea no one else had ever thought of before what do you do i develop a batch manufacturing system used for making pharmaceuticals food and beverages and specialty chemicals think about making cookies i have a recipe to follow and i have some ingredients i measure my ingredients and follow the recipe step by step to make a batch of cookies i add the ingredients in a specific order i have equipment i need to use like a mixer mixer bowls an oven that needs to be preheated to the proper temperature i may be able to tweak my formula or ingredients to make peanut butter cookies instead of chocolate chip cookies and create a whole new cookie or product different batches of products have recipes for each product and each recipe has a formula and special equipment the batch is run on however the recipes are large in scale and complex why is the future important i have two kids with a third on the way so when i think about the future i often think of them we should be making the world a better place for them to live and thrive solve todays problems as well as the problems of the future how does your job make tomorrow better and safer than today we help make products we use every day that are critical to our wellbeing the solutions we offer allow our customers to ensure that the products they release for consumers are of the highest quality and meet regulatory standards the medicine you take every morning is 100 percent safe and effective the milk you drink and the food you eat is guaranteed to be manufactured following a consistent repeatable process which has been validated and tested to ensure it is of the highest quality as our population grows doing this in a more efficient and costeffective manner which allows customers to serve the growing demands of the market what do you for fun i enjoy spending time with my family my husband zach and my sons nate and harrison they keep me pretty busy i also enjoy physical fitness and being outside running walking and playing field hockey in the summers we
