refrigeration makes modern life possible solstice refrigerants are widely used and provide low or reduced global warming potential versus the alternatives here are some of the researchers engineers and other leaders who invent make and commercialize honeywell solstice refrigerants and help keep our homes and cars cool in hot weather our food supply from spoiling during transportation and our vaccines safe at cryogenic temperatures ali bergeron based geismar louisiana what do you do i manage two refrigerant units why does refrigeration matter apart from allowing us the luxury of having air conditioning in our cars and homes as well as to extend the life of certain foods refrigeration is necessary to keep vital medicines such as insulin and vaccines viable for people all over the world what advice would you give to someone who wants to follow in your footsteps dont doubt your abilities and take advantage of every opportunity afforded to you along the way even if it means taking a chance in a role you may not have seen yourself in elizabet vera becerra location buffalo new york what do you do i research and test new heat transfer fluids in equipment which give cold temperatures like the machines that makes ice cream or the refrigerators in our houses or the systems that provide cooling when it is too hot outside why does refrigeration matter refrigeration helps to conserve in safe conditions food in our houses and during transportation from the farms to the supermarkets it also helps preserve prescription pills from pharmaceutical plants to pharmacies and hospitals what advice would you give to someone who wants to follow in your footsteps be willing to accept that it is going to be teamwork you will work with many people of different areas of knowledge learn different areas in science thermodynamics chemistry physics mathematics instrumentation algorithms and basic business principles tafa magaya based morris plains nj what do you do i am director of offering management in fluorine products which means i make sure that we achieve both short and longterm revenue and margin targets why does refrigeration matter refrigeration helps to preserve many important things so we can transport them safely and or preserve them for later consumption or use what advice would you give to someone who wants to follow in your footsteps id tell them to be themselves know what you want and go for it work hard but never compromise your principles in the process victor alvarado based geismar louisiana what do you do i am an engineer for the solstice yf production i do the math behind the scenes and come up with cool ideas to make the production better and safer why does refrigeration matter refrigeration is what makes us able to have cool tech literally it helps with the ac in your car homes school etc what advice would you give to someone who wants to follow in your footsteps for engineering you need a good balance of creativity and knowledge if you think you would like coming up with ways to make something better and be fully involved with how to make it then maybe engineering is for you work hard in school focus on your math and science and give it your all thats how good engineers are born sunita rai singh location morris plains nj what do you do i work with cool rd people and smart business development folks who work day in and out to develop and commercialize technologies that keep this planet cool safe and sustainable why does refrigeration matter as the climate gets hotter and urbanization spreads to the growing population there is going to be a need for more comfortable living and willingness to pay for these comfort solutions so much of urbanization and supply chain is now dependent upon the ease of keeping food fresh longer storing and moving medicines and drugs farther making habitat comfortable even in sever weather conditions and all of this is made possible because of available refrigeration technologies developed by innovative companies like us what advice would you give to someone who wants to follow in your footsteps i wanted to work for a company that had a direct and tangible impact on making this planet safer and sustainable and at the same time was an exciting place to be with all its innovations so i chose to join honeywell if you value innovation and like to contribute
