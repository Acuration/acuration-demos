unpredictable and significantly enhancing data security for utilities end users no other companies in the smart meter market currently offer quantumhardened encryption protection the integration of this technology into honeywells smart energy and thermal solutions products will help address the industrys growing data security needs particularly as it relates to critical infrastructure robust
