everything at home now as a director of engineering he helps pilots operators maintenance workers and passengers stay connected at all times based in shanghai china here is his futureshaper story what brought you to honeywell honeywell was at a campus recruitment event i attended as fate would have it honeywell was my first and only employer what gets you excited about going to work every day i work with a great talented team plus honeywell gives us the opportunity to grow and be successful with the business we have in china if you were a fortune reader what would you predict as the future of your industry i would predict that flying a plane will be as easy as driving a car fixing a plane will be as quick as fixing a car being a passenger on a plane will be as seamless as sitting in a cab in your eyes what qualities does a futureshaper possess a futureshaper is always positive actively learning and growing has an adaptable mindset and the ability to embrace and celebrate changes what advice would you give someone who wants to follow in your footsteps believe in yourself grow from all your experiences it makes you who you are when you are not working what do you do for fun i
