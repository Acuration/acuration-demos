together and honeywell volunteers enrich lives of grove park residents in atlanta october 18 2017 atlanta oct 17 2017 prnewswire honeywell nyse hon volunteers and rebuilding together a leading national nonprofit that rebuilds homes and communities for people in need throughout the country today partnered to help revitalize five houses in the grove park neighborhood of atlanta during the daylong event 50 honeywell volunteers made critical home repairs and improvements including painting and stabilizing iron railings removing debris weatherizing doors and windows and other renovations in the homes the effort is part of a community service initiative with rebuilding togethers atlanta affiliate which coordinated the project and honeywell hometown solutions the companys corporate citizenship initiative which provided 50000 in funding we need to ensure longterm residents of the grove park neighborhood are not displaced by investors said caroline blakely president and ceo of rebuilding together through honeywells support we can provide homeowners with the critical repairs they need to maintain their homes and help them age in place we are excited to take on our first project with rebuilding together atlanta less than five miles from our new midtown atlanta offices said anne folk vice president human resources and communications honeywell home and building
