honeywell building technologies launches nextgeneration digital tools to meet evolving customer needs new learning system enhances customer training experience across product categories new ecommerce portal improves buying process with personalized realtime information february 22 2021 atlanta february 18 2021 honeywell nyse hon a global leader in connected buildings today unveiled two virtual platforms designed to improve its customer experience touchpoints and better meet customers needs the platforms my honeywell buildings university a new online learning management system to expand customer training and mybuildings honeywellcom a new ecommerce portal that delivers a more seamless purchasing journey are focused on the channel partner audience including distributors system integrators and commercial customers were creating an endtoend digital ecosystem with the aim to make it easier for our customers and channel partners to do business with honeywell said andy kupsco vice president of customer experience for honeywell building technologies these new tools are the first capabilities in our new digital customercentric experience focused on providing better product education and training as well as simplifying the buying process weve worked hard to deliver comprehensive personalized and easytouse platforms that answer our customers most pressing needs my honeywell buildings university enhancing the customer training experience myhoneywellbuildingsuniversitycom helps customers develop skills and gain knowledge associated with honeywell fire security and building management system bms products the interactive online learning platform offers unprecedented access to honeywells industry expertise and certified training courses through a curated library of ondemand content that delivers recommendations tailored to the individual customer customers can browse a global catalogue of training modules access ondemand training and selfhelp videos register for instructorled sessions and review training history the platform provides users with more visibility into the breadth of honeywells training resources and control of their individual training journey on my honeywell buildings university customers can navigate by productor solution through an intuitive user experience enroll in instructorled courses access to an expanded library of ondemand content earn productcertifications log on to an evergrowing library of honeywellbacked content my honeywell buildings university currently supports 20 honeywell brands and offers more than 700 hours of ondemand content includes more than 200 instructorled courses and is accessible for customers on any device in any location and at any time mybuildingshoneywellcom simplifying the customer buying experience mybuildingshoneywellcom is a new ecommerce portal that provides customers with a more direct buying process through access to holistic realtime information the platform is easy for customers to use customize and access and streamlines the endtoend purchasing journey so they have more time to focus on driving business value the onestop portal allows customers to browse products from the honeywell product catalogue check pricing and availability place an order check the latest order and shipment status as well as submit and track support cases each customer will have a personalized dashboard with realtime information once users are registered on the platform they will have the opportunity to establish accounts for other members of their team and even control information access on individual accounts key features include complete product details and specifications detailed product pricing availability and inventory customizable kpi dashboard accurate order placement and tracking simple returns and support the platforms are currently available to north american distributors that sell honeywell fire security and bms products and then open to other markets and customers globally it will grow to include the entire honeywell building technologies value chain new features and functionalities such as selfserve tech support capabilities and knowledgebased
