brings smiles to rural communities of fulgaon village panchayat on international day of happiness honeywells anand gaon project brings smiles to rural communities of fulgaon village panchayat on international day of happiness the initiative aims to enhance the lives of rural communities of fulgaon village panchayat 20 march 2023 india march 20 2023 honeywell hometown solutions india foundation hhsif the philanthropic arm of honeywell nasdaq hon has successfully completed the redevelopment of the first village fulgaon under its project anand gaon this project is an initiative by honeywell to improve the quality of life for the rural communities in the vicinity of honeywell entities by providing infrastructure support and basic amenities fulgaon village panchayat of maharashtra was adopted in the year fy 202223 as the first village under the project anand gaon today on the day of uns international day of happiness honeywell conducted the handover ceremony of anand gaon this is under the holistic and sustainable community development thematic focus of hhsif the hhsif partnered with americares india foundation aif and the staff of hhsif and aif with the support of local leadership at hail plant in fulgaon conducted a need assessment and interacted with the panchayat members to understand the requirement based on the request and assessment study honeywell earmarked resources to upgrade the school and provided solar streetlights to the village and solar panels to the panchayat office and make the school energy efficient the upgraded amenities will put a smile on the face of 300 rural children and will impact the lives of 10000 population the refurbished school and other facilities were unveiled and handed over by mr ashish gaikwad managing director honeywell automation india limited on behalf of honeywell to mrs mandakini sakore president gram panchayat fulgaon we are proud to have partnered with americares india foundation and the local leadership at hail plant in fulgaon to complete the project anand gaon said ashish gaikwad managing director honeywell automation india he also added through this project we have upgraded the school provided solar streetlights to the village and solar panels to the panchayat office to make it energyefficient which will have a positive impact on the lives of 10000 people in the community it is our privilege to hand over these upgraded facilities on the international day of happiness we believe that businesses have a responsibility to support the communities they operate in especially those living in rural areas honeywell remains dedicated to creating a sustainable and inclusive future and the anand gaon initiative is a testament to this commitment anand gaon is a significant milestone for honeywell and its commitment to improving the quality of life in the communities where it operates meenakshi batra country director americares india foundation said our partnership with honeywell will help the redevelopment of the first village fulgaon under its project anand gaon with this collaboration we aim to make a difference by holistic and sustainable community development
