defined as widely known and acknowledged especially for distinctive excellence according to merriamwebster dictionary when it comes to buildings iconic structures are game changers representing nations religions and economies they interlace past societies with current cultures they facilitate transportation and invention from physical design to behindthescenes technology these features make a building iconic architecture arches columns domes and spires define skylines sublime skyscrapers stretch into the clouds and massive sports arenas witness victory and defeat a buildings architecture years later today the buildings house two museums a national library and theatre connecting visitors with hungarys art and history technology behind the scenes at these timeless structures is modern technology that makes it possible to host visitors who can focus on their experiences at the sydney opera house 82 million people a year enjoy performances every year and the building adjusts to
