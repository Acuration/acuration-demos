with intelligent programmable wall outlets and lights to extend energy savings to guestroom lighting the thermostat senses when guests enter the room and instantly communicates with lighting controls via a 24ghz rf to activate a welcome environment when the room is unoccupied the lights and outlets are turned off within a 30minute time period to save energy spectre meets title 24 guest room occupancy controls requirements for installations to support property compliance the spectre solution communicates wirelessly is easy to install and requires no new cabling this makes it ideal for
