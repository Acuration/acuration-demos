standards processes and procedures cmmi is a performance enhancement model consisting of collected best practices that guide organizations to improve key capabilities and elevate performance hts appraisal at cmmi dev v20 level 5 represents a commitment to high quality software development processes and delivery standards and translates into far superior and highly reliable solutions for honeywells customers we view being certified as cmmi dev v20 maturity level 5 as an important differentiator providing us with a competitive advantage that will help us achieve our longterm business objectives added pratap the cmmi dev v20 maturity level 5 appraisal enhances hts capability to develop and deliver new products for honeywell and to lead the companys eastforeast localization strategy
