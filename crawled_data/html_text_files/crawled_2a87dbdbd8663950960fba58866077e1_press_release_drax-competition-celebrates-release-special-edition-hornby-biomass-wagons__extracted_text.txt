a vip tour of the biomass unloading facilities three runner up winners will each receive the limited edition hornby biomass wagon set drax processes and compresses low grade wood into small highdensity pellets turning humans oldest renewable energy source into a fuel that is modern and highly efficient the majority of the compressed pellets are imported from the huge working forests of north america which are expanding year on year draxs new energy source is made from the residues and thinnings left from industries that have no use for them our specially designed trains move millions of small pellets from four uk ports  hull immingham liverpool and tyne  to our power station with a minimal cost and environmental impact hornby has released two special edition model wagons
