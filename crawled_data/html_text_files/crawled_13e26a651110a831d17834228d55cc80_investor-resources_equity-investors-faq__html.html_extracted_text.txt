Equity investors FAQ
Investors FAQ
Here’s a list of some useful questions (and answers) for anyone investing in, or who might be thinking about investing in, Drax Group plc.
On which exchanges is Drax Group plc listed?
Drax Group plc shares are listed on the London Stock Exchange (LSE).
What is the stock symbol for Drax Group plc?
The stock symbol for Drax Group plc on the LSE is DRX.
What is the ISIN number for Drax Group plc?
The ISIN number is GB00B1VNSX38.
What is the nominal value of Drax Group plc ordinary shares?
The nominal value of Drax Group plc ordinary shares is 11 16/29 pence each.
Who are the Company’s registrars and how do I contact them?
The name and contact details of the Company’s registrars are as follows:
Equiniti Limited
Highdown House
Yeoman Way
Worthing
West Sussex
BN99 3HH
Telephone: From within the UK 0371 384 2030 (calls cost 10 pence per minute from a BT landline, other providers’ costs may vary). From outside the UK 371 384 2030.
You should contact Equiniti Limited directly if you have a query which relates to any of the following:
Change of name or address
Lost or stolen share certificate or dividend cheque
Death of the registered holder of the shares
When contacting Equiniti Limited it would be helpful if you had your investor code to hand and also be sure to quote Drax Group plc in any communication.
How do I transfer my ordinary shares to another person?
You will need to complete a stock transfer form and send it to Equiniti Limited, together with your share certificate(s), to arrange the transfer of ownership.
Please note: Where the transfer is for a cash consideration you will need to send the stock transfer form to your local HM Revenue & Customs office for stamping. For further information, please contact either your local HM Revenue & Customs office or Equiniti Limited.
Do you have a low-cost share dealing service?
Drax Group plc does not at present have a share dealing service for shareholders and prospective shareholders. You should therefore contact a bank or a stockbroker to buy or sell our shares.
Do you have a corporate Individual Savings Account (ISA) facility?
No.
Is there any way I can donate ordinary shares to charity?
If you would like to donate some or all of your Drax Group plc shares to charity, you can do so by contacting ShareGift at:
4
th
Floor Rear, 67/68 Jermyn Street, London, SW1Y 6NY
Telephone: 020 7930 3737
Website: sharegift.org

I have not received my last dividend cheque. What should I do?
Please contact Equiniti Limited, who will investigate the reason for non-receipt.
You may wish to have further dividends paid directly into your bank or building society account. Equiniti Limited will advise how you can do that.
I have an out-of-date dividend cheque. What can I do?
Drax Group plc dividend cheques may be cashed within 12 months of issue. After this time, they need to be reissued. You can do this by returning the cheque to Equiniti Limited. To avoid this problem, you should consider having your dividend cheques paid directly into your bank or building society account. Equiniti Limited will advise how you can do that.
Can I take my dividend in shares?
Drax Group plc does not offer this option.
Is Drax Group plc a member of the FTSE4Good Index Series?
Yes.
