Carbon Removals by Drax

Supporting you to decarbonize
Drax supports companies to go beyond their sustainability goals
or reducing emissions
by
also removing historic and current CO2 emissions and permanently storing
them
.

Bioenergy with Carbon Capture and Storage (BECCS) by Drax will generate millions of tonnes a year of high integrity, permanent carbon removals in the U.S. as early as 2026.

In 2022, Drax
announced the world’s biggest Carbon Dioxide Removals deal
, and since then numerous forward-thinking, companies have signed-up to collaborate with Drax.

Case Studies
Respira is an impact-driven carbon finance business leading the way in the voluntary carbon market. They support companies in their mitigation strategies by providing high-quality carbon credits and support the deployment of critical technologies like BECCS by Drax, and the resulting CDRs. Find out more
here
.
Ana Haurie, Respira International CEO and co-founder said:
“We are proud at Respira to be leading the way in the voluntary carbon market, supporting companies in their mitigation strategies by providing high-quality carbon credits. The deployment of critical technologies like BECCS by Drax, and the resulting engineered CDRs, very much have their place as an important instrument in the value chain management which supports corporate action”
C-Zero supports companies and organisations with renewable energy and emission reduction solutions as they navigate the pathway to net-zero carbon by 2040. They help businesses to identify Net Zero objectives, examine their green credentials and define a pathway with clear milestones and budgets. Their diverse team has decades of experience in understanding climate risks and opportunities in specific sectors. Find out more
here
.
Bruce Brown, Head of Compliance Contracts said:
“We have been helping our clients navigate through the UK energy markets transition for some time now and it is fantastic to be able to support them and be able to offer BECCS as part of the solution.”
