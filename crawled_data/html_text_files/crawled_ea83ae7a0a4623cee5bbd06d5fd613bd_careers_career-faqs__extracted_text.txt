cruachan power station visit drax power station home  careers  career faqs career faqs frequently asked questions about careers at drax group thinking about working for us take a look at the following faqs how do i apply for a vacancy when you find a vacancy that interests you the application process is simple select apply now for the position you wish to apply for then work through the registration and application process for the role alternatively if you have previously registered on our current vacancies website  log in using your email address and password and select apply now for the position you wish to apply for when youve completed the necessary sections youll need to accept the terms and conditions and this will submit your application you will receive an email to confirm that your application has been submitted how do i reset my password if you cant remember your password select login enter your email address and use the option to reset password if your address is found well send you an email with a randomly generated password youll then need to log in using this password but will be prompted to change this to something more memorable if youre still having problems please contact the relevant recruitment team using the contact details at the bottom of the vacancies page  how do i find out about new vacancies all our vacancies are advertised on our current vacancies website  on this page you will see a link called create job alert by clicking this you can set up a job alert based on the types of role you are looking for as well as the location in which it is based once a job alert has been created you will receive an email each time a job matching your search criteria is posted if youd like to unsubscribe from these emails at any time you can do this by logging into your profile and managing your settings what does drax expect from me we expect you to do your best if you apply for a job here we anticipate that youll spend some time to find out about the company  this might be from our website or from any other source that may help you make your application relevant to the job and include a covering letter explaining why you would like to be considered we expect successful applicants to be good performers to have a good attendance record and to demonstrate our values  what happens to my application after i have applied once you have submitted your application youll receive an email to confirm that your application has been received we will then start our shortlisting process if you are logged into our current vacancies website  you can track the status of your application by logging in and viewing your current applications what are the next steps if i am shortlisted if you are shortlisted we will contact you to let you know the next step in the process  this will depend on the role you have applied for as part of the recruitment process you may be asked to participate in an interview testing eg aptitude personality verbal and numerical reasoning and in some cases be asked to attend an assessment centre where could i be working the majority of our uk workforce is based at drax power station which is located near selby in north yorkshire it is within commuting distance of towns and cities such as leeds york doncaster and hull with motorway links to the m62 m1 and a1 however there may be roles in other locations including london cardiff ipswich northampton argyll and bute dumfries and galloway and lanarkshire please ensure you check the details of the vacancy before applying what happens to the information i give on the diversity page the information is used to monitor the effectiveness of our diversity and equality policy  and to ensure our recruitment process is fair your data will be kept confidential within hr and may be used in summary reports to management teams trade unions and employee representatives but these reports do not identify individuals does drax work with recruitment agencies we work with a number of recruitment agencies that we have agreed terms and conditions with if you are a recruitment agency and would like the opportunity to work with us in the future please contact the relevant resourcing team using the contact details at the bottom of our current vacancies website with a brief overview of your agency the area you specialise in and your contact details if we are interested in working with you we will get in touch we do not accept emailed cvs  these must be uploaded to our current vacancies website what if i have a query about the progress of my application please log in to our current vacancies website and view your application where youll be able to see the status of your application if the status is pending then we are still processing your application for some of our vacancies we receive a large number of applications and therefore the initial selection process can take a number of weeks or months we will always send you an update on your application even if you have not been successful we therefore ask for your patience during this process what security checks will i undergo the law to prevent illegal working requires all new employees to produce an accepted original form of identification guidance on this will be included in any formal offer of employment we also require two satisfactory references and documentary evidence of all the academic and professional qualifications and registration with professional bodies listed on your cv will i have to complete a medical questionnaire if you progress to receiving a formal offer of employment you will be asked to complete a medical assessment questionnaire your completed questionnaire will then be returned directly to our occupational health team who may follow up with a medical assessment if appropriate do you provide relocation assistance we have a relocation assistance policy which is applied where appropriate if i am not successful will you let me know we will always contact you to let you know the outcome of your application usually via email please also check your junk email folder as sometimes our systemgenerated mails can be classed as junk by your provider if i would like feedback on my application who can i speak to we are unable to provide feedback on individual applications and cvs if you attend an interview it will be possible for you to receive feedback how do i register my interest in working for drax you can register your details and cv on our system by creating an account by doing this it means we can search for your relevant skills and qualifications when suitable vacancies arise it also enables you to set up email alerts for when we post a new job see above how long will my details remain on the system your details will remain on our system for 12 months in line with data protection laws if you do not log in to your account for 11 months youll receive an email to notify you your account has been deleted if you receive an email letting you know what your account with expire in 30 days you can reactivate this simply by logging back into your profile related stories press releases drax welcomes
