Transforming energy for businesses

We help businesses improve electricity purchasing to mitigate risk, reduce cost, and increase sustainability. How businesses buy energy should account for today’s increasing price volatility, emerging opportunities from renewables, and significant regulatory changes. A strategic approach to energy purchasing and risk management

We help businesses optimize, understand and better manage their energy consumption in a more efficient and sustainable way.

 

We provide energy purchasing and sustainability strategies including:

  • Renewable Energy advisory
  • Competitive sourcing and negotiation of electricity and gas contracts
  • Progressive purchasing management and risk management services
  • Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs)
  • Sustainability goals 
  • We also have have a world-class Utility Bill Management service.
