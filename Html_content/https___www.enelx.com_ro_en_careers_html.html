Careers

Are you interested in technology and how it can turn energy into new opportunities? Your place could be with us! We continue to recruit top specialists, but also young people at the start of their careers. 

 

If you share our values - responsibility, innovation, confidence, proactivity - and are looking for a professional environment that is vibrant, generates new ideas and embraces diversity, we want to hear from you.

 

Discover jobs and internship programs, available within the company, on recruitment platforms - BestJobs, eJobs, LinkedIn - and on social media. And if the job you're looking for isn't among those advertised, you can send us your CV to recrutare.ro@enel.com.

 

Among the benefits we offer are a hybrid work schedule, medical benefits, extra days off based on seniority, an international work environment where internal mobility to new roles is encouraged, and many other development opportunities.
