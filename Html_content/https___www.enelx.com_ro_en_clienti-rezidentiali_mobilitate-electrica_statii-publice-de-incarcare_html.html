Meet our public charging stations

When you're on the move, you can rely on our public electric or hybrid plug-in charging stations on city streets and roads across the country.

How it works

  1. Download the app
  2. Create an account
  3. Find a station on the map
  4. Charge the car

Download the JuicePass application directly on your smartphone and get access to all Enel X electric charging services. 

 

Sign up with your personal data or using your Facebook or Google account.
Navigate the map to find the nearest charging station. 
Start the charging session in the application and track in real time the charging time and power.

Why use Enel X public charging stations

Easy to use

Manage all recharging services using your personal smartphone.

A growing network

The Enel X network is constantly growing: our stations can be found in parking lots belonging to hotels, supermarkets or shopping malls.

Sustainable products

Our stations are sustainable, their components being made of recycled plastic.
