Demand Response Calculator

Find out how much your company can earn by curtailing its energy consumption or production.

Step 1/4

The calculation takes only 5 minutes and requires 3 basic inputs.

To estimate your potential earnings with Demand Response, enter your:

  1. Location
  2. Industry
  3. Load Curtailment

The data submitted will only be used to perform the calculation and does not need to be accurate to provide an estimate of the result.
