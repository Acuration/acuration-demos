interested in peaking response learn more workplace ev charging program learn more resources south australian backup boost program weve partnered with the south australian government to support local businesses by changing the way they use energy enel x is building a 20 mw portfolio of dispatchable capacity in south australia with 2 million in funding support from the south australian government the program supports backup generator upgrades and the installation of battery energy storage systems bess across a number of south australian businesses to help unlock the potential for cheaper more reliable electricity the program will connect businesses backup generators and bess to support grid stability by participating in enel xs demand response programs and being part of our virtual power plant vpp its designed to deliver more affordable and reliable electricity to south australian households and small to medium businesses the works are being partially funded by the south australian governments backup boost grant with no requirement to repay accelerating sites entry into the market the remaining costs are recovered from revenue generated from participating in our vpp to learn whether your business qualifies get in touch with our team enabling businesses to supply the electricity market will give them a new revenue stream and deliver lower prices for everyone else it is commonsense to make use of resources that are currently underutilised for the benefit of both the owners of the assets and the wider community dan van holst pellekaan mp minister for energy and mining
