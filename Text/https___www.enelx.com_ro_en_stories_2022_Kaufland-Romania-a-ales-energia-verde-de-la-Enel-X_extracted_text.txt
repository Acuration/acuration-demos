in sustainability in our country with a network of 152 stores and 2 warehouses nationwide the company constantly invests in renewable energy by equipping its network with photovoltaic panels in 2021 for the fourth consecutive year kaufland romania ranked first in the top of the most sustainable companies nationwide a title awarded by the cst index the distinction is awarded on the basis of an independent analysis that evaluates romanian companies according to a series of international parameters depending on their level of involvement in the field of sustainability and social responsibility challenge kaufland romania is committed to taking action and implementing projects that contribute to the achievement of the un sustainable development goals global compact which address the most pressing issues on the planet from eradicating poverty improving health and education increasing urban sustainability and upholding the unrecommended principles of governance in the field of human rights anticorruption environmental protection and sustainable working practices at the same time the company has assumed new targets for reducing greenhouse gas emissions following the schwarz groups accession to the science based targets initiative targets that are in line with the level of decarbonization needed to limit global temperature rise to 15  c in order to increase its degree of sustainability  energy efficiency and to achieve the objectives assumed by the schwarz group kaufland romania has chosen the partner enel x for part of the investments in innovative green technologies this first project of the company in romania meant the implementation of a solution for local electricity production using over 2000 photovoltaic panels and will expand the existing capacity in the near future the system generates green energy and helps reduce the negative impact on the environment eliminating over 270 tons of greenhouse gas emissions annually the substantial savings achieved since the first year of use of the photovoltaic plant but not only helped the company make the decision to continue this type of projects and the predictability obtained ensures stability in the current increasingly dynamic business environment solution the project involved the installation of a photovoltaic system for the kaufland logistics center in turda by installing the following enel x solutions on the ground over 2000 photovoltaic panels with halfcell monocrystalline cells on an area of over 9400 square meters on the ground in the immediate vicinity of the logistics center 8 sma string inverters generating 110 kw power prefabricated metal structures  specially designed for photovoltaic installations located on the ground monitoring system  sized for optimum energy management monitoring and stability solar panel savings calculator estimate how much your business could save with a solar photovoltaic plant in terms of energy costs and co2 emissions find out more benefits reduction of annual consumption enel x solutions help the company cover over 193 of the total annual electricity consumption of the warehouse and reduce carbon dioxide emissions by 272 tonsyear the photovoltaic panel system has an installed capacity of 1 mwp  which can generate a total quantity of electricity of approximately 1277 mwh per year reducing energy costs annual electricity savings estimated at over 167000 euros in the first year of operation  process monitoring by constantly monitoring onsite and offsite processes enel x tools contribute to maximizing the energy generated increasing the operational reliability of the photovoltaic system minimizing downtime in case of failure and extending the life of the photovoltaic system through this approach we want to expand sustainability actions and the fight to protect the environment the project helps reduce the impact on the environment by lowering the carbon footprint green energy which comes from renewable sources is a sustainable alternative and helps maintain a clean environment therefore the companys goal will be to invest in the future in such solutions with our partners valer hancaș  communication and corporate affairs director kaufland romania for us this project was a new opportunity to strengthen our position as one of the most important integrators of energy systems which increase the competitiveness and sustainability of our customers the process of installing the photovoltaic system for the kaufland logistics center was an efficient one being completed within the agreed deadline the decision to use canadian solar panels was made based on the strong history we have with this manufacturer as well as the excellent priceperformance ratio which is the key to
