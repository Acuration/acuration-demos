energy location sacheon south gyeongsang province client eew korea sector manufacturing on august 16 2022 enel x korea signed a rooftop solar power generation agreement with eew korea and started commercial operation in november last year the rooftop solar power plant located at eew korea is the second rooftop solar power plant owned by enel x in sacheon region enel x korea is responsible for installing managing and operating the rooftop solar power plant on the roof space provided by eew korea the client eew is a global leader in the offshore wind equipment pipe manufacturing sector it has production bases in sacheon south gyeongsang province and gwangyang south jeolla province domestically with an annual production capacity of 150000 tons eew holds iso 9001 certification as well as api 2b and api 5l certifications recognizing its excellence in production capacity quality and business management in the global market additionally eew holds iso 14001 certification demonstrating its commitment to environmental management and ecofriendly operations the agreements benefits 1523 kwp of solar capacity 94717 tons of coâ‚‚ emissions avoided annually 2672 solar panels comprising the pv plant enel xs challenge the need the solution the benefits eew korea aimed to enhance its ecofriendly image and contribute to net zero through renewable energy generation eew korea wanted to install solar facilities in their idle unused roof space within its facility through the solar facilities eew korea wanted to supply ecofriendly energy to the power grid preventing an increase in atmospheric co 2 emissions to fulfill eew koreas needs enel x korea has undertaken the installation of solar facilities on the roof of eew koreas factory in sacheon south gyeongsang province utilizing a total roof area of 22711m 2  2672 modules have been installed producing up to 152304 kwh of renewable energy per hour the generated energy is sold to the power grid and the revenue from energy generation is shared between eew korea and enel x korea operating the power plant under a 20year contract a yearly production of 2151 mwh of renewable energy is achieved this leads to an annual reduction of 94717 tons of carbon dioxide emissions equivalent to planting 8500 pine trees each year this project marks a significant step towards decarbonization which is one of enel xs global and local objectives the project will promote the use of renewable energy while generating new revenue for eew korea to offset rising electricity costs or to reinvest in other sustainability initiatives hyoungmin kim country
