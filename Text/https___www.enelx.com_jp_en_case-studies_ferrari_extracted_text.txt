automotive the need ferrari wants to increase its selfproduction of energy and reduce co₂ emissions as part of an environmental and economic path towards sustainability carbon neutrality is the ultimate goal the solution to meet ferraris needs enel x designed a solar photovoltaic pv system that will be installed on the rooftop of ferraris famous factory in maranello italy boosting the companys selfproduction of energy while reducing co₂ emissions emissions was the main goal of the project the solar pv system comprised of more than 3800 solar panels will have a maximum capacity of 1535 kwp that will cover part of the ferrari premises electricity consumption in addition enel x will create a solar pv parking canopy that is completely off grid  combining the solar with a battery storage system to meet the charging needs of ferraris new hybrid range and future fullelectric vehicles the charging performance will be monitored and shared through enel xs energy management system which will display the data in realtime the benefits once running at full capacity the entire project will save more than 18500 metric tons of co₂ over 25 years the plant will be able to generate 1626802 kwhyear helping to reduce emissions and meet the companys carbon neutrality goals 1535 kwp of maximum capacity 740 tons of co₂ emissions avoided yearly 1 fully offgrid carport its a source of
