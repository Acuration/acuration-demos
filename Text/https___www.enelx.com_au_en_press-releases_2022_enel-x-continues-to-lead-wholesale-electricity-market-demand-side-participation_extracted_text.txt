market events skyrocketing electricity prices and businesses facing high electricity retail rates now more than ever businesses are looking to reduce their cost of energy savvy energy users are extracted were seeing diverse sectors capitalise on this opportunity including grocery and cold storage operators data centres agriculture and manufacturing these organisations recognise the importance of harnessing their flexibility to support sustainability objectives by enabling more renewables and replacing coal generation today marks the oneyear anniversary of the wholesale demand
