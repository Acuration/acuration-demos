partners go to page stories ontario energy storage calculator see how much your organization can save march 27 2019 energy storage enables large commercial and industrial ci energy users to consume the lowestcost electricity at the times when consuming from the grid is most expensive in ontario this is a big opportunity for many large ci energy users in the province the global adjustment ga charge represents as much as 70 of the electricity bill for those that participate in the industrial conservation initiativewhich is available to all facilities with a peak demand of 1 mw or greater as well as those in certain industries with a peak demand of 500 kw or greaterannual ga charges are calculated based on the facilitys average demand level during the ontario grids five highest onehour periods of electricity demand throughout the previous year those that can anticipate when these events will occur and reduce this level of demand by 1 mw will save about 500k in ga costs over the following year learn more with our indepth whitepaper understanding ontarios energy storage opportunity to help ontarios ci energy users get an idea of the potential impact the technology could have on their energy costs the enel x north america team developed an interactive calculator tool with inputs to specify the results based on building type energy consumption needs and even ongoing efforts
