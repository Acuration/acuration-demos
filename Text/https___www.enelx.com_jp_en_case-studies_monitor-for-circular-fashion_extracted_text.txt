monitor for circular fashion case studies of activities with italian fashion industry about circular fashion what we do the benefits location italy client na sector fashion this case study is based on a project developed in enel x italy about the monitor for circular fashion the fashion industry is one of italys most strategic economic sectors and among those with the most significant impact on the environment therefore it needs to become a key driver of the circular economy if we want a more sustainable future to contribute to creating a more sustainable economy  sda bocconi and enel x have teamed up to produce the monitor on circular fashion an overview that provides clear visibility into the circular maturity level of the italian fashion industry  what we do the monitor on circular fashion covers a representative crosssection of the italian fashion industry across the entire value chain  reporting on best practices and gaps and proposing models and methods for integrating circular approaches into the business cycle  in its first year 2021 enel x created a circular economy report that summarized the companys energy assessment conducted oneonone sessions and created a tailored roadmap of solutions to improve the level of circularity the second year 2022 of activity builds on these foundations by working with 16 brands retailers and service providers throughout the upstream and downstream supply chain as well as a kpis committee made up of consultants who are experts in measuring circularity the pilot projects focused on supply chain partnerships in which monitor companies will mentor and support young designers and italian landmark entities to share values through circularity the pilot projects also tested the principles of the unece united nations economic commission for europe sustainability claims for the third year in 2023 the project was significantly upscaled and presented three projects consisting of an open innovation approach with 26 partners including tods group ferragamo  and vivian westwood  the benefits companies that take part in brainstorming sessions receive an sda bocconi research report as well as an individual enel x circular economy report  with both corporate and sitespecific energy analyses the circular economy report is an important tool that pinpoints areas for improvement  provides a roadmap to circularity and decarbonization and enhances corporate communication strategies our longterm goal is not only to improve the circularity of the italian fashion industry through this project but also to start the same outside of italy sda bocconi monitor for circular fashion education research and brainstorming for a more circular way of doing business learn more renewable energy 1 case study 1 featured
