What is JuicePump?
The fast charging solution, which paves the way for electric mobility outside cities and makes it accessible to all.
Complex
Allows charging of two electric or hybrid plug-in vehicles at the same time, having the three standard connectors (CCS, Chademo, Type 2).
Flexible
It is suitable for urban areas, in shopping malls, as well as outside localities.
Connected
Due to GPRS/3G/4G communication protocols, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi and LAN, control and maintenance can be performed remotely.

Useful tips
An Enel X consultant will offer you the perfect solution if there is a need for fast charging of electric or hybrid cars: in the city or outside it, in commercial buildings or on service areas along the roads.

The installation of a JuicePump charging station for electric vehicles is done quickly and safely, with the help of the included accessories.

You manage charging stations from one place, through the various communication protocols installed. Provide access to electric car drivers through the JuicePass app.

Made of sustainable materials, the JuicePump can charge 2 vehicles simultaneously through the three DC and AC standard connectors.

Your advantages
Real-time assistance
JuicePump communicates in real time with the Control Center (EVOs) via UMTS/LTE/ETHERNET/WIFI protocols. Remote technical support is available 24/7.
Total Safety
The anti-vandal system protects the station when in standby mode. The housing is weather resistant, being ideal for outdoor installation.
