Solar, Storage and EV Charging at UMass Boston
UMass Boston, the iconic public university in Massachusetts, is working with Enel X to bring renewable energy plus storage to its urban campus. In addition, the partnership will see 11 Juice Box smart chargers from Enel X’s e-Mobility product line installed throughout the campus. This project demonstrates the breadth of innovative technologies and services Enel X provides to customers that deliver cost savings and manage their energy needs, while also uniquely serving as a tool for students through hands-on learning opportunities.
