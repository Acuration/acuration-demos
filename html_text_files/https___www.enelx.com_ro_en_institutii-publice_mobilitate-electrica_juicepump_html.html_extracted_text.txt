The fast charging solution, which paves the way for electric mobility in and outside cities and makes it accessible to all.

How it works
Consultancy
Full control
An Enel X consultant will offer you the perfect solution if there is a need for fast charging of electric or hybrid cars: in the city or outside it, in commercial buildings or on service areas along the roads.
The installation of a JuicePump charging station for electric vehicles is done quickly and safely, with the help of the included accessories.
You manage charging stations from one place, through the various communication protocols installed. Provide access to electric car drivers through the JuicePass app.
Made of durable materials, JuicePump can simultaneously charge through the three DC (AC) and AC (AC) standard connectors.
Your advantages
Real-time assistance
JuicePump is always connected to the Control Center (EVOs) via UMTS/LTE/ETHERNET/WIFI protocols, and the remote technical support service is available 24/7.
Total Safety
The anti-vandal system protects the station when in standby mode. The housing is weather resistant, being ideal for outdoor installation.
