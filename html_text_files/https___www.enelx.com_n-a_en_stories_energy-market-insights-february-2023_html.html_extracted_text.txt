Energy Market Insights for February 2023
Our Enel X team analyzes the latest headlines and price movements in North America.
February 15, 2023
In the February Energy Market Insights, our team of analysts offers their monthly breakdown of the stories, products and price movements in North American energy markets. To find out more,
download the full report
, which covers topics like:

New England
: Find out the latest in pricing, including why 2023 forwards are nearing 12-month lows and how warm weather has affected January 2023 LMPs.

New York
: New York has announced a variety of new energy programs for 2023. Find out how they could affect energy prices and decarbonization.

Mid-Atlantic
: Virginia’s recently proposed legislation would have effectively eliminated third party supply. Learn more about why customers need to stay aware of similar bills in the future.

Midwest:
Discover why Ohio utility default rates are set to jump on June 1, and what customers can do to minimize the impact.

California
: Learn why the expected retirement of four California peaker plants this year could lead to increasing price volatility in the future.

Texas
: See how the early February ice storm in Texas affected pricing, and how the grid responded.

Mexico
: Find out the latest on pricing and the capacity market in Mexico.

Natural Gas
: Get a rundown of the pipeline projects that are expected to come online this year throughout the United States.

Renewable Energy:
Read our breakdown of the RE100 2022 Annual Report and its biggest takeaways.
