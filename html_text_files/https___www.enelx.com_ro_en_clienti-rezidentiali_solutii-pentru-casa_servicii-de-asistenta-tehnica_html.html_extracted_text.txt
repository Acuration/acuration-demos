Your Benefits
Unique number 031 9987
Problems with switches, plugs and sockets? Leaking or clogged pipes? Central heating blockages or air conditioner malfunctions? Now you have the same number for the electrician and the plumber and the central heating or air conditioning repairman. And even the locksmith. Call 031 9987 available 24 hours/ day and you have access to a network of technicians covering the whole country.
Specialized technicians
You can rely on a team of skilled and reliable technicians to intervene promptly in the event of an incident. All you have to do is call 031 9987 and our operators will take care of your request.
No worries
Don't let the unexpected surprise you. With our technical support services, you're covered for mandatory checks on your heating unit and gas installation, as well as coverage for various home and office repairs.
