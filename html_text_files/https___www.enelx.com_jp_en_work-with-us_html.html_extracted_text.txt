Work with Us!
We wish to deliver energy to society in a new, sustainable way, a way that will open up new possibilities for our customers and lead the way to new experiences. We want to be innovative, challenge ourselves, and change society. We seek your passion for the future of energy to lead the transformation to a digital and sustainable future.

Our support
We know that we need to be in the best physical and mental condition to continue taking on challenges. Enel X is working on various systems and environmental improvements to ensure that our employees can work in good health and comfort.
Care leave
Five days of paid leave per year for employees to care for themselves, people they wish to care for, pets, and things.
Birthday leave
One day of special paid leave that can be used within the employee's birthday month.
Paid Maternity Leave
Employees are paid the same salary equivalent to working their regular working hours for up to 84 days from the starting date of the maternity leave.
Club Activities
Employees initiate and receive predetermined subsidies. Currently, a golf club, sauna club, tennis club, darts club, and English club are active. New club activities are welcome.
Defined Contribution Pension Plan
All employees are eligible.
Skill Improvement Training
External training is available to support employees in acquiring the knowledge they need or will need in the future to promote their work and improve their skills.
