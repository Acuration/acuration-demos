How it works
Consultancy
The charging process
Enel X consultants are at your disposal to create sustainable solutions for charging electric cars for local administrations, residential centers or public parking lots.
The creation of an infrastructure for the fleet of electric cars is done quickly and safely, with the accessories included.
Electric car charging stations can be managed from a single portal. Employees with electric cars receive quick access through the JuicePass app.
The advantages of the JuicePole are multiple: the high-power dual AC outlet allows the simultaneous powering of two electric cars and the charging of the battery up to 40% in just 30 minutes.
Your advantages
A connected city
JuicePole meets the needs of a city to have fast and affordable outdoor electric vehicle charging options.
Intuitive, urban design
JuicePole has a modern, elegant design, adaptable in urban environments. The parts have anti-theft technology and ensure easy maintenance of the ports.
