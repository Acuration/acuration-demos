A Trusted Partner for Your Energy Transition
Embracing a carbon-free future begins with a plan—and a partner that can make it happen. Enel X is the trusted partner of federal, state, local and provincial governments across North America. Enel X’s advisory team helps plan and execute smart, low-carbon energy solutions.
Putting the Pieces Together
We help public sector clients turn their decarbonization goals into action. From renewable energy workshops that identify opportunities and build consensus to an emissions reduction playbook leveraging state-of-the art technology and distributed energy solutions, Enel X is your sustainability partner.

Nobody Does It Better
Enel X’s public sector partnerships have resulted in numerous awards and a  track record of procuring low-cost, renewable power. Working with Enel X means partnering with experts who have experience navigating the complexities of the public sector while delivering energy management outcomes.
