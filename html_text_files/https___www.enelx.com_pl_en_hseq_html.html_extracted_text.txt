Our values
Enel X believes that integrity, transparency, and compliance are essential to running a sustainable energy business. Accordingly, the company has aligned itself with the highest international standards. Our management system for policies, procedures and guidelines is designed to align Enel X branches around the world to provide all clients with clean, efficient products and services.

Certifications earned by Enel X
Enel X branches around the world adopt local management systems based on ISO-certified standards and guidelines issued by our headquarters, and reported in each Enel X country’s website.
ISO 9001
Ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer needs and applicable statutory/regulatory requirements
ISO 14001
Ability to uphold environmental sustainability, performance and compliance as a management strategy
ISO 45001
Ability to provide a safe and healthy workplace, eliminate hazards, and proactively improve occupational health and safety performance
ISO 37001
A management system is in place to detect, prevent, and combat bribery, and to comply with anti-bribery laws
