JuiceNet Set&Charge
A simple solution to monitor and manage charging stations for electric vehicles, located in private areas with limited access.

What is JuiceNet Set&Charge?
How it works
Your advantages
What is JuiceNet Set & Charge?
JuiceNet Set & Charge is a complete solution through which a public administration can provide access to authorized users to the private charging network of electric or hybrid plug-in cars.
Stations control
You monitor the ongoing and completed charging sessions and you have access to full data from each charging station.
Access for drivers
You allow authorized users to access charging stations through the JuicePass app.
You set the price
The portal offers full control over the price grid as well as real time monitoring of the charging sessions.
Optimizarea consumului
You optimize charging based on available power and limit power for a specific group of charging stations.

Main page
Useful information
You can quickly view all the essential information and data related to the charging points of the institution, as well as the activity of the users.
Details of each electric or hybrid car charging session, performed at your stations, depending on the user.

Details about drivers' performance: numbers, status, charging sessions, consumption.
Details about the performance of charging points: total consumption, availability. In addition, you can control your charging points directly from the dashboard.
An interactive map with the places where the charging points are installed.
Your advantages
You are independent
You can customize your EV charging infrastructure. No matter what your electricity supplier is, this service is independent and does not require additional cables.
Charging points ownership
With JuiceNet Set & Charge service, the charging stations become the property of the institution. Choose the suitable range: JuiceBox Pro & Pro Cellular, JuicePole or JuicePump.
