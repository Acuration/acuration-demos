Energy Market Insights for July 2022
The Enel X team of analysts breaks down the stories and price movements across North America.
July 20, 2022
In our July Energy Market Insights, our team of analysts offers their monthly breakdown of the stories and price movements in North American energy markets. To find out more,
download the full report
, which includes analysis of stories such as:

New England
: Eversource NH default rates are soaring. Learn how customers on utility default rates in Eversource NH should react.

New York
: Find out the biggest stories from NYISO’s 2022 Power Trends Report, including supply concerns and capacity market changes.

Mid-Atlantic
: PJM held its 23/24 capacity auction after major delays. Find out why some of the results may bring a welcome reprieve to customers.

Midwest
: Find out why Michigan Public Service Commission’s approval of Consumer Energy’s IRP could provide a significant capacity margin buffer for MISO Zone 7 through 2025.

California
: Find out more about California’s new Grid Reliability Fund, the state’s latest attempt at addressing unsustainable grid reliability risks.

Texas
: Extreme hot weather has arrived in Texas and low wind generation is putting stress on the grid. Find out how this could affect end users.

Mexico
: Find out why the Federal Electricity Commission’s tariff subsidies may lead to long-term uncertainty.

Natural Gas
: Read the latest on the effects of tumultuous gas prices in Europe.

Download the full report to find out more!
