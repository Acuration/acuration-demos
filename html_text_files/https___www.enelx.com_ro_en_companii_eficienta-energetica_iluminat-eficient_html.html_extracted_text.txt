What is efficient lighting?
Not only residential clients consider the consumption of the light bulbs when it comes to energy savings. Companies value savings just as much and look more and more often for smart solutions for indoor and outdoor lighting.
At Enel X we integrate cutting-edge lighting fittings in smart systems, which can be monitored through digital platforms. This opens the way to transparency and optimization, with financial and decarbonisation benefits.
New or existing office?
Our experts will transform it inside and out, using efficient best in class LED lighting solutions for companies.
Impressive lifespan
The smart LED lighting systems we offer will provide a quality light, with a lifespan of up to 50,000 hours.
Smart technologies
The innovative LED products we use consume little energy, are easy to maintain, safe and easy to integrate in smart installations.
