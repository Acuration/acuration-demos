Inside Gillette Stadium’s Smart Energy Strategy
Gillette Stadium partnered with Enel X to create an optimal strategy to maximize demand response earnings, consolidate and manage monthly bills for more than 80 utility accounts spanning the stadium and Patriot Place, and provide a more sustainable experience for fans installing over 50 EV chargers in the parking lot.
