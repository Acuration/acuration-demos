Why Enel X
Smart EV Charging Solutions
Supporting employee adoption of EVs and electrifying fleets and is providing businesses a rare win-win opportunity: enhancing ESG credentials while driving down operational costs. Enel X is the only partner that can provide the complete electrification solution of EV charging,  distributed energy resources, renewable electricity supply and Virtual Power Plant integration, to achieve your electric mobility goals at the lowest cost.
Smart EV Charging Programs for Employees
Growing EV adoption requires smart EV infrastructure to match. Enel X helps businesses quickly gain the clean-energy benefits of a smart charging infrastructure to support, and turbocharge, employee EV adoption. Enel X Way’s line of commercial smart EV charging stations comes with easy-to-use asset management software and versatile installation options designed to easily fit into any parking footprint. We offer unique financing options, to learn more, contact us.
Reduce Costs with Smart Fleet Solutions
Public and private fleet operators are switching to EV fleets to reduce costs, cut emissions, and lower their carbon footprint. Whether you operate a light, medium or heavy-duty fleet, EV fleets and smart EV charging solutions reduce operating costs over their lifetime. Our global vehicle fleet customers include Fleet Management Companies and Fortune 1000s in industries such as transportation, biotech, telecommunications and utilities.
1,500+ fleet vehicles electrified
Enel has electrified 1,500+ fleet vehicles with the goal of going 100% electric by 2030. Find out how Enel X can help you design a fleet electrification program with its partner Enel X Way.

Why Enel X and EV charging?
To deliver smart business and employee charging, Enel X partners with Enel X Way - a global leader in smart electric vehicle charging solutions with over 320,000 charging ports worldwide. As a subsidiary the Enel Group, Enel X Way is committed to providing smart mobility solutions for drivers, businesses and partners to make driving electric simple.
