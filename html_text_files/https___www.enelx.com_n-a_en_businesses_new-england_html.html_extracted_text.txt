New England Energy Solutions
Clean Energy Solutions to Reduce Your Energy Costs and Environmental Impact
Birthplace of the American Revolution and now cradle of clean energy innovation, New England offers a number of lucrative energy opportunities. At Enel X, our menu of energy and cost-saving solutions includes: solar-plus-storage, demand response for businesses and utilities,  backup generator upgrades, UBM, and energy supply management.

Energy Storage In Massachusetts
SMART Incentives for Distributed Energy Resources in Massachusetts

The SMART program is an incentive for solar-plus-storage projects that represents Massachusetts's commitment to promote more renewable energy in the commonwealth. See how advanced energy storage solutions can help you lower your energy costs while tapping into this incentive program and others—without any up-front capital.
