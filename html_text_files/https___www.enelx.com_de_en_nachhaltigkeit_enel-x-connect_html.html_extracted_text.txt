What is it?
Enel X Connect is a simple, accessible and effective digital energy suite that combines all our digital solutions. Enabling all-in-one user-friendly management of your energy consumption via the efficiency of big data-based software, articficial intelligence and machine learning, Enel X Connect reduces the time your company needs to spend on its energy management.
All-in-one
With Enel X Connect, you can access all our software solutions using your unique login credentials.
Data comparison
Detailed energy data allows you to understand the cost drivers, minimize risks and pinpoint areas for efficiency.
Greater sustainability
Define and monitor your sustainable energy strategy through electrification, renewables and circularity.
