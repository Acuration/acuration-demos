What is it?
Our electric mobility Partner Program provides a structured channel designed to engage and support you throughout your collaboration with Enel X. Whether you are looking to grow into this innovative sector or to consolidate an established poisition, we offer thorough commercial and technical support so that you can operate effectively in your market and successfully meet customer expectations.
Collaboration
Our resellers and installers are critical to our success. We are always eager to work with innovative, reliable and proactive partners.
Purchasing
Our procurement team takes care of the supply chain, playing a key role in identifying the most effective solutions.

Evaluate & agree
We assess your eligibility and prepare a partnership agreement for you to sign.

We offer three different levels of engagement: X Partner, X Partner Pro and X Partner Prime with the same structure and objectives. Each level gives access to different benefits and training paths. The Partner Program clearly identifies technical and commercial requirements to qualify for each engagement level.
Train & certify
Develop a sales plan. Receive professional training for products and brand:

Technical Training
Our mandatory technical courses which end by testing the knowledge and skills acquired, to enable the certification and get you started. Certification is mandatory to sell JuiceBox, JuicePole and JuicePump. The training program covers a wide range of topics, from hardware installation and maintenance to software configuration.

e-Mobility Academy
Our set of general courses designed to improve your commercial effectiveness. These courses are aimed at improving Enel X brand awareness and providing an overview of the e-Mobility sector and technologies.

On completion of these training course will be certified as an Enel X electric mobility Partner.
Place your first order
Congratulations. You’re ready to sell our range of best-in-class charging solutions to your end users as well as services such as installation, maintenance, and after-sales support.

As a certified partner you gain access to a marketing package containing digital and physical support materials, such as leaflets, gadgets and more.

Based on your performance you will be able to unlock further fundings to be used for marketing and communication activities, such as holding events, producing relevant materials and more.
