Reduce Costs and Manage Risk Through Strategic Energy Supply Management
In a complex and constantly changing energy market, it’s more important than ever for commercial and industrial energy consumers to be prepared for anything.
Enel X assists large energy consumers in creating and executing custom energy supply management strategies to reduce energy costs, manage risk, and adapt to new market trends and developments.
How does it work?
Custom Supply Strategy
Advisory Services

Energy Supply Strategy Development
Our supply management energy services establish the optimal energy supply strategy for your business. Metrics like your organization’s finances, risk aversion, and sustainability priorities are all included in our calculations.

Live Reverse Auctions and Sealed Bids
We work as an energy consultant on our client's behalf to solicit bids and encourage competition from all available suppliers, securing the best price for your energy purchase.
Relevant Insights for Your Strategy
Receive regular updates on the market and regulatory developments that could affect your energy spend. Complete with detailed analysis, these metrics indicate how your business will be affected and how your organization should react.

Demand-Side Optimization Services
Our energy management services provide you with direct access to energy industry experts so you can work with our expert advisors to evaluate demand-side optimization efforts. At Enel X we develop and employ strategic operational scheduling and on-site distributed energy resources in order to reduce exposure to high energy costs.
Renewable Energy Strategy Development
Establish your objectives and execute a renewable energy procurement and purchasing strategy to maximize environmental and financial impact. Use your access to your Enel X energy advisor to manage risk within traditional energy markets.

Simple and Accurate Carbon Impact Reporting
Easily report portfolio-wide emissions data to account for renewable purchases to meet all regulatory, industry, and custom standards.
