Useful tips
An Enel X consultant will offer you the perfect solution for charging plug-in electric or hybrid cars for shopping malls, business parks, residential centers, public car parks or your company's car park.

The installation of a car charging station or the creation of an infrastructure for the fleet of electric cars or plug-in hybrid is done quickly and safely, with the help of all the included accessories.

You manage charging stations in one place. You provide user access with electric cars or hybrid plug-ins through the JuicePass app.

Construit din materiale durabile, JuicePole are o putere de 22 kW per priză și este compatibil cu orice vehicul electric sau hibrid plug-in.

Your advantages
Real-time assistance
JuicePole communicates in real time with the Control Center (EVOs) via UMTS/LTE/ETHERNET/WIFI protocols, in case you need assistance.
Total Safety
JuicePole is equipped with an anti-vandal protection system that protects the station and sockets when in standby mode.
