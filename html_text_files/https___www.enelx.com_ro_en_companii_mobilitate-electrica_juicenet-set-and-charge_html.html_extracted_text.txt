How it works
Your advantages
What is JuiceNet Set & Charge?
JuiceNet Set & Charge is a complete solution that gives authorized users access to your private network for charging electric or plug-in hybrid cars.
Stations control
You monitor the current and completed charging sessions and have access to the station data, individually or per group of stations.
Access for drivers
You allow authorised users to access charging stations through the JuicePass app.
You set the price
Through the portal you have control over the price grid for charging sessions, which you can monitor in real time.
Consumption optimization
You optimize charging based on available power and limit power for a specific group of charging stations.

Your advantages
You are independent
You can customize your EV charging infrastructure. No matter what your electricity supplier is, this service is independent and does not require additional cables.
You own the charging points
Once you have chosen the JuiceNet Set & Charge service, the charging stations become yours. Choose the most suitable range: JuiceBox Pro & Pro Cellular, JuicePole or JuicePump.
