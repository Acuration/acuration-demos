High-performance photovoltaic panels from Enel X, funded by the Electric Up program
The year 2020 has come with good news for Romanian entrepreneurs and, why not, for all those interested in environmental safety: the Electric Up program, a large European funding project for SMEs and HORECA companies. Under this program, the state finances the purchase of solar panels to produce electricity - photovoltaic panels to power charging stations for electric and hybrid cars, with a maximum amount of €100,000.

If you're interested in the program and want to apply for funding for your project, now is the time to learn more about Enel X photovoltaic panels. From the features and benefits of photovoltaic panels to their installation, find out everything you need to know below.
Solar or photovoltaic panels - the future of electricity systems
Globally, more and more countries are focusing on environmental responsibility: plug-in electric cars and alternative energy sources are the way to a safer and cleaner future for the whole planet. Photovoltaics is a modern technology that converts solar radiation into electricity. Exploiting a natural and inexhaustible source of energy guarantees a high level of flexibility, even in the event of variations in consumption influenced by your business typology.

The average lifespan of such a system is over 20 years, making the installation of photovoltaic panels not only a decision with a positive impact on the environment, but also a strategic financial decision that can help you run a more profitable business in an increasingly dynamic market.
If you also want to apply for funding in the
Electric Up program
, it is worth considering the photovoltaic systems offered by Enel X, which are aimed at all types of businesses interested in exploiting a technology of the future.

What are the benefits of photovoltaic solar panels
Companies that opt to install solar panels for electricity generation can optimize their costs by reducing the impact of electricity price increases. A photovoltaic system brings a number of benefits to any company:
Autonomy in electricity generation
Increased flexibility and energy independence
Creating a responsible identity in the marketplace through involvement in lowering the carbon footprint
Lower electricity costs, higher profit in the end
Possibility to obtain international certifications, which is a clear advantage in dealing with customers and business partners
In addition, through the Electric Up program you have the chance to pay back your investment and enjoy all these benefits by getting your company's proposed project financed. To apply to the program implemented by
Ministry
of Economy, Energy and
Business
Environment
(currently the Ministry of Energy)
, it is recommended that you read the Applicant's Guide beforehand, where you will find all the steps to apply for your project.

Why it pays off to install high-performance photovoltaic panels from Enel X
If you want to have the best chance of installing solar panels with European or government funding, you need a reliable partner who can provide you with the high quality products and services needed for both a successful project and a successful future business.

The installation of photovoltaic systems from Enel X means, above all, the guarantee of a future partnership. You'll benefit from expert advice to help you identify the right solution for your business, and you'll have a dedicated team of specialists ready to assist you with planning, installation, testing and grid connection.

On the solar panel side, Enel X offers you photovoltaic panels from world-class manufacturers with the following advantages:
The capacity of the panels is between 385 Wp and 440 Wp, depending on the result of the technical expert assessment
State-of-the-art technology gives you high efficiency of at least 18% per module
The resistance is high, up to 5400 Pa for snow loads and up to 3600 Pa for wind loads
12-year manufacturer's warranty
Long service life - over 20 years
Choose to invest in the future of your company by investing in a state-of-the-art energy solution that contributes to environmental safety and helps you gain autonomy in electricity generation.

Install photovoltaic panels from Enel X and enjoy a profitable and sustainable business!
