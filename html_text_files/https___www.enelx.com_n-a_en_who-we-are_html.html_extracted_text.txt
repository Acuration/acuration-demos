Who We Are
Enel X North America combines the spirit of a startup with the infrastructure and expertise of a global innovator to provide smart value-added services and solutions that enable businesses and communities to create, store, use, and manage energy more efficiently, sustainably, and strategically.
WATCH THE VIDEO

What We Stand For
We make opportunities happen by enabling the transformation of energy. We stand for reducing global emissions and helping organizations navigate a complex energy market.
Our Vision
A world where businesses and communities are empowered to grow and thrive in a clean, circular energy economy.
LEARN MORE
Our Mission
Become a leading producer of sustainable, innovative, simple technology solutions for navigating the energy transition.
LEARN MORE
Circular Economy
Eco-design-focused technological solutions centered around renewable energy and recyclable materials.

Meet Our Head
Enrico Viale
Enrico Viale was appointed Head of Enel North America in July 2019. He previously served as the Enel Group's Head of Global Generation (from July 2014) and Head of Global Thermal Generation (from April 2016).
