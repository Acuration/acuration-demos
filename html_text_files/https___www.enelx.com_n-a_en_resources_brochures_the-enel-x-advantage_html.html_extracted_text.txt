The Enel X Advantage
Demand Response Participation in Ontario
DOWNLOAD PDF

With over 200 sites in Ontario alone, Enel X helps organizations across the Province earn money, save on energy costs, and mitigate carbon emissions with demand response (DR).

The Market Leader

Enel X has the depth and breadth of experience to maximize its partners’ IESO DR earnings. Enel X has participated in DR programs worldwide since 2003 and in Ontario since 2008. We consistently hold the largest market presence in the IESO DR Auction, with a 29% share of the demand response market.

Strong Regulatory Presence

Enel X also maintains a dedicated regulatory affairs presence in Ontario. We work closely with national, regional and provincial regulatory agencies to support what is in the customers’ best interest.

Real-time Energy Management

Enel X provides you with access to your real-time energy data—giving you the information you need to perform your best during demand response events and to better manage overall energy costs.

Our experts are ready to coach you to ensure you are meeting your demand response targets.
