What do I do if there is a car occupying a charging station, even if my App flags it as available?
Users who have completed their charge must clear the area to make way for others. Enel X is not responsible for any obstructions. If a car is blocking your access, we suggest you report this to the relevant authorities.

If the charging station goes into an offline state, does it operate in a fail-safe mode?
If data connectivity is lost the JuiceBox will not enter a fail-safe mode, the JuiceBox will continue to operate/charge at the selected current value previously set within JuiceNet dashboard or the JuicePass App.
