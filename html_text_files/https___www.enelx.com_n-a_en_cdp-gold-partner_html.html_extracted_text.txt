Enel X’s Renewable Energy Solutions Are Gold-Accredited by CDP
As a CDP Gold Accredited Solutions Provider for Renewable Energy, Enel X can better help companies plan custom-fit sustainable energy roadmaps that meet corporate sustainability goals and objectives.

CDP
is a global non-profit organization that runs the world’s environmental disclosure system for companies, cities, states and regions. Together, Enel X and CDP promote transparency in environmental performance data.

Enel X offers advisory throughout the decarbonization process, including:
Power purchase agreement (PPA) sourcing
Renewable energy strategy and implementation
Carbon and emission reduction roadmapping
Carbon accounting and resourcing tracking
Stakeholder engagement

Enel X has helped customers secure 3 GW of PPAs over the last three years and 60,000 GWh of renewable energy over the past 16 years globally through its in-house
Energy Exchange platform
.
This partnership will bring highly valued expertise to the many thousands of companies using CDP to disclose... We are confident that Enel X's capabilities can support companies to implement more ambitious renewable strategies.
Paul Robins
Head of Corporate Partnerships, CDP
