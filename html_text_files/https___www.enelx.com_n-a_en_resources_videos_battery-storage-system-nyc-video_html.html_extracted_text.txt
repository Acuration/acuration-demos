Enel X and Related Companies Install New York City's Largest Battery Storage System
Enel X and Related Companies installed New York City's largest battery storage system in Related’s Gateway Center in East New York, Brooklyn. The system will support the local grid of New York energy company, Con Edison, in periods of high peak demand, ensuring service remains reliable. Read more in the
