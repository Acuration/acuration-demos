JuicePass App
JuicePass is our digital product that allows you to manage all recharging services using your personal smartphone.
What is JuicePass
Install the app
How it works
What is JuicePass?
Enel X JuicePass is a mobile application through which you can manage all the charging services of an electric vehicle at public and private charging stations.
Simple
You can easily register in the application using your personal Facebook profile, your Google account, or as an anonymous user.
Personal profiles
You can choose the most suitable profile for your personal needs: private, corporate and special.
Everything in one app
You can see the charging stations map, station type, access schedule & costs, and you can reserve a connector.
Universal
With JuicePass you can access all Enel X compatible public stations, but also private stations at home or in the office.
JuicePass App
Download now the Enel X JuicePass application on your phone.

How it works

Technical Support
Tariffs - tariff plans
1
Connect
Connect
Download the Enel X JuicePass application from iOS Store or Google Play.
Sign up with your personal data or using your Facebook or Google account.
Register as a private user or as a business, by entering the VAT code of the company.
You can also use the service as an anonymous user without creating an account.
2
My garage - car info
My garage - car info
Add all the information about your electric vehicle for an even more customized charging experience.
You will be able to choose each time you charge the car at public stations or at home.
Filter charging stations on the map to see only those that are compatible with your car.
3
My wallet - choose the tariff plan
My wallet - choose the tariff plan
Choose the tariff plan you prefer and complete the account creation by adding a payment method.
With the tariffs available in the application, you can access the charging services, choosing between a tariff paid as you go or a predetermined monthly tariff.
If you have a code for your company, you will be able to activate the tariff plan chosen by your company.
1
Station map - identify the station
Station map - identify the station
Find on the map the charging station closest to you.
The Enel X network is always expanding, but you can also recharge with our partners.
Our stations can be found in hotels, supermarkets, malls and can be identified according to the specific pin.
The color indicates the status of the charging station: green - available, red - in use, gray - under maintenance.
2
Charging stations - how to use the station
Charging stations - how to use the station
Open the station details section to find out: address, serial number, accessibility, maximum power and distance.
Choose the right connector for your car and check the price.
Technical issues? Press the Support button and we will be ready to help!
Once the connector is selected, press the “Charge" button and start the session.
Book the right connector through the booking service.
3
Public recharging - the charging session
Public recharging - the charging session
"Swipe to charge" and start the charging session in the next 90 seconds.
On the screen you see in real time the number of units (kWh) charged, the duration of the session and the charging power.
You can stop the charging session at any time by simply pressing the "Stop" button.
The session details and upload history can be found in the “Charging History" section of the application.
1
Recharging at home
Recharging at home
Connecting the home charging station to the application is easy: click on the icon in the upper right corner and start the integration process!
Follow the steps in the application and quickly set up the charging station configuration on your mobile phone.
With Enel X JuicePass you can manage and plan the charging at home. Select only the desired days and times and connect the car.
2
History
History
You can access the charging history whenever you want, using the side scrolling menu. View the number of units (kWh) charged in a week, month, or time period of your choice.

3
RFID Card
RFID Card
The RFID card is an alternative tool that allows you to charge an electric vehicle without using the mobile application. Purchasing one is easy, simply enter the payment method and mailing address for delivery.
Connect it to your account and you're ready to recharge. For Android smartphones, the card is also available in a digital version!

Premium fare / consumption

3
Basic X
Basic X
Fixed fee of RON 75 / month = 60 charging units (60 kWh).
If you exceed 60 units, the rate in the Basic Payment/Consumption plan is automatically applied.
Unit price: RON 1.69, semi-fast charge with AC (alternating current) and RON 2.09, fast charge with DC (direct current).
Unlimited access to the reservation service + any type of connector. The invoice in PDF format is emailed to you and is available in JuicePass, in the "My Account" section.

The units included and unused cannot be transferred to the following period or transferred between subscription plans or to other customer accounts.
The switch from a subscription with KWh included to another subscription or product will be done at the time of the request. The units included in the previous subscription and unused will be lost once the switch has been done.
4

Premium X
Fixed fee of RON 140/month = 120 charging units (120 kWh).
If you exceed 120 units, the Basic Payment/Consumption plan automatically applies.
Unit price: RON 1.69, semi-fast charge with AC (alternating current) and RON 2.09, fast charge with DC (direct current).
Unlimited access to the reservation service + any type of connector.
The invoice in PDF format is emailed to you and is available in JuicePass, in the "My Account" section.

The units included and unused cannot be transferred to the following period or transferred between subscription plans or to other customer accounts.
The switch from a subscription with KWh included to another subscription or product will be done at the time of the request. The units included in the previous subscription and unused will be lost once the switch has been done.
