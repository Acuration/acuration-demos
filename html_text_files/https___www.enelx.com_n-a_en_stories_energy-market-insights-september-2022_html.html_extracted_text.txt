Energy Market Insights for September 2022
The Enel X team of analysts breaks down the stories and price movements across North America.
September 20, 2022
In our September Energy Market Insights, our team of analysts offers their monthly breakdown of the stories and price movements in North American energy markets. To find out more,
download the full report
, which covers topics like:

New England
: New England governors and ISONE alike have looked to federal authorities for winter fuel security solutions. Find out if any relief is on the way, and what the coming months may hold.

New York
: NYISO capacity forwards dropped below $2/kW-month for winter 2022-2023. Find out how this could impact customers with sites in New York.

Mid-Atlantic
: Basis price spikes are causing even more price pain for natural gas contracts in the Mid-Atlantic. Find out what effects recent construction could have.

Midwest
: FERC has approved MISO’s new seasonal resource adequacy construct. Find out how this could help address risk in the region.

California
: California set a new demand record on September 6, demonstrating once again its susceptibility to heat storms. Find out what happened, and what this may mean for the future.

Texas
: ERCOT has set a slew of demand records in 2022, and future energy prices have doubled compared to a year ago. Find out where ERCOT may go from here.

Mexico
: Learn about the Supreme Court’s decisions on the recent natural gas order of the Energy Secretary.

Natural Gas
: As hurricane season continues, find out more about how these storms could affect natural gas prices.
