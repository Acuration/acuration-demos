Enel Group in Numbers
Enel Group, headquartered in Rome, Italy, operates renewable energy businesses in 29 countries globally. We were the first company in the world to introduce smart meters, and today we are the largest renewable energy company in the world. Since its establishment, we have been constantly introducing new technologies to improve energy reliability, lower costs, and provide sustainable energy.

Enel X Japan
Enel X offers a range of solutions to achieve a sustainable society, facilitating the transition to a decarbonized and circular economy. We will create new value by combining the Enel Group's more than 60 years of experience in the energy business with cutting-edge energy infrastructure and digital technology.
Our vision
A new energy for a new era. We are committed to the transition to sustainable energy.
Our mission
Sustainability, innovation, and digitalization are revolutionizing the world's energy picture. Enel X will lead the world in this transformation.
Message from Country Manager
「We provide decarbonized energy solutions as a strategic partner to help our customers achieve ESG goals toward the ideal energy use of the future.」
Enel group is headquartered in Italy and provides a wide range of energy solutions in 30 countries around the world.
The global movement toward a carbon-free society is accelerating with practical actions by governments and companies, and there is a growing need for new services that integrate energy infrastructure and digital technology.
Enel X Japan provides a variety of decarbonized energy solutions such as Demand Response in the Japanese market, based on our experience and achievements in the U.S. and European markets, , which are leading the way in the digital application of energy solutions, with "energy infrastructure" x "digital" x "financial" x "carbon neutrality" concepts.
We will continue to stay close to our customers and create new value in order to contribute to the realization of a sustainable, carbon-neutral society.
Thank you for your continued support and cooperation.

Hiromitsu Miyamoto
Country Manager, Enel X Japan
