Who We Are
We are leading the change, accelerating the energy transition.
Enel X Singapore
Backed by Enel, one of the world's 100 largest companies, we were established to be a factory for innovative energy-related services. Enel X is leading the transformation of the energy sector around the world.
In Singapore, we are helping businesses decarbonize, digitalize and electrify energy use to drive sustainability and reduce costs.  We do this with our world-leading Virtual Power Plant platform. We are the global market leader in this space, managing a 24/7 VPP across five continents, with 7.7 GW of DR resources.
Our global resources, energy market experience and technological expertise positions us uniquely to help Singaporean businesses transform energy into new opportunities.
