How it works
Your advantages
100% electric public transportation
Enel X has created a complete e-City solution that includes charging infrastructure installation and management services (green buses, batteries and charging stations), fleet management and consumption optimization.
Greener cities
Reduction of CO2 emissions and noise pollution, as well as better air quality.
Lower costs
Savings of up to 70% on energy consumption and reduction of operational costs, with a positive effect on public finances.
Efficient and sustainable
Energy from 100% renewable sources, fleet management and consumption optimization.
How it works
Decarbonization
Our solution
Local partnerships
After successful implementation in the private and business environment, Enel X can offer sustainable transport solutions to public administrations, thus accelerating the transition to electric mobility.

Public administrations benefit from a complete, flexible and innovative solution that includes services for the installation and management of the electric charging infrastructure and the electric bus fleet, as well as the optimization of consumption.

Enel X can build strong partnerships with key public transport operators, such as electric bus suppliers, to provide integrated solutions for the supply, management and maintenance of the vehicle fleet.

Your advantages
Sustainable public transportation
Enel X's portfolio of solutions offers public administrations an efficient, economical and sustainable public transport service, without affecting investments.
A new experience for citizens
Enel X's innovative and sustainable solutions are specially designed to transform the experience of citizens in public transport. The waiting time becomes profitable.

Sustainable solutions for your city
Enel X equips cities and companies with smart tools to efficiently manage their electric fleets.
