Development Program for Suppliers
The Supplier Development Program comes to support the growth of local suppliers by identifying the expansion paths that best fit their business objectives.
The program provides an ecosystem of opportunities, offering free access to:
financial services (reverse factoring)
training (in the fields of safety, sustainability innovation, sustainable development, circular economy)
consulting
dedicated business offers for energy supply and smart energy products

The Supplier Development Program is addressed to all suppliers in the energy sector but also in sectors of interest for us, which want to develop their business in areas such as: Safety, Quality, Environment, Human Rights and Correctness, Innovation, and Collaboration.

The initiative targets both current and potential suppliers that offer non only electrical works but also design and civil works services, IT services to help implement Enel’s digitalization process, call center services, communication and media services, human resources development services, as well as support services such as cleaning, security, and recruitment.

The participation in the Program is free of charge while interested suppliers must only register by e-mail to
qualificationenel.romania@enel.com
for take advantage of the benefits.

The Supplier Development Program is our initiative that seeks to encourage the sustainable development of our suppliers so that the company’s generation chain is stronger, more efficient and more innovative, and to ensure that our strategic objectives are met, given that suppliers play a crucial role in achieving the energy transition targets.

For any kind of support and inquiries, suppliers can send an e-mail to: qualificationenel.romania@enel.com.
