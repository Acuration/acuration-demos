We catalyze transformation through energy in Romania
Technology is propelling energy to new horizons. We offer simple, innovative and scalable solutions.
"As of October 25, 2023, the assets of Enel X Romania have become part of the portfolio of Public Power Corporation (PPC), leader in the energy sector in Southeast Europe, and the name of the company is now PPC Advanced Energy Services Romania."
Tangible innovation
The way we use electric energy today opens up opportunities for people, companies and public institutions. PPC Advanced Energy Services România brings these opportunities closer, through fast, integrated energy efficiency solutions that make energy consumption sustainable for customers while responding to ever-changing needs to make them more competitive.
We generate opportunities
We transform energy into services and we offer a wide range of solutions that customers can use to improve their offices, cities, homes and lives.
We aim to bring this opportunity to all, by stimulating opportunities for connection, collaboration and innovation. We connect people, urban ecosystems, business sectors and electrify transport for a smarter life.
We inspire you to use energy in new ways
We are leading the revolution in new uses of energy, through an integrated and flexible business strategy aimed at encouraging change. We facilitate customer development through technologies that generate value and protect the planet.
We want to accelerate the transition to sustainable mobility, conscious, efficient consumption, to the generation and storage of electricity from renewable sources. This will help companies, cities and people to live, work and grow in a sustainable way.
For people
We transform the quality of life through safer, more efficient, more comfortable and more sustainable housing.
You will take control of your home and you will be able to optimize the use of electrical appliances.
You will discover the advantages of driving an electric car and the ease of using public or personal charging stations.
DISCOVER
For companies
We transform companies for the benefit of customers, employees, shareholders and the environment.
The solutions we develop and refine, combined with our experience in the field, will open a new path to greater energy efficiency and energy independence. We identify new opportunities to reduce costs, generate new value flows and develop more sustainable organizations. We support you with dedicated services that reduce financial risks.
DISCOVER
For institutions
We transform cities into a cleaner and more sustainable ecosystem for the benefit of citizens.
With the help of the technologies we use and our experience, you manage smart and sustainable cities. We work together and find the right solutions for lighting and smarter use of energy in public buildings and beyond. We also offer mobility solutions that will improve the lives of residents and expertise in accessing development funds.
