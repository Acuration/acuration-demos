myenergi named in startups 100 the annual list of most disruptive new uk businesses






we are honoured to have been named in this years startups 100 index




established in 2008 the startups 100 index identifies privatelyowned uk companies launched in the last five years that demonstrate innovation solid financials economic impact and the ability to scale








run by 
startupscouk
 the programme is the longest running index of its kind previously including brands such as revolut deliveroo hellofresh and bulb






since we were founded in 2016 we have skyrocketed through the industry continuing to experience huge global demand our unique range of renewable energy products for ecosmart home management is what has made this global expansion possible allowing us to open subsidiaries all over the world become the 
uks 1 best selling electric vehicle charger
 and recently ship our 100000th unit


 






jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented our inspiration has always been to create a positive impact push the boundaries of possibility and change the world through pioneering green technologies we started with nothing except for a few good ideas and a serious passion for change  today were widely considered a global market leader






being named in the startups 100 index is truly a real honour considering the challenges of the past 18 months its a privilege to be recognised for our innovation scalability and the success story that weve created






richard parris managing editor of startupscouk added after a particularly difficult year for businesses we are happier than ever to celebrate uk success stories every year the startups 100 shows how companies are positively responding to the issues concerns and trends of the time
