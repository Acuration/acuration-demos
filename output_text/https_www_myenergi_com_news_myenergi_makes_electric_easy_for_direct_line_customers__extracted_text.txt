myenergi makes electric easy for direct line customers
 


 








 










customers who insure their car with direct line will now be able to save money on the purchase and installation price of a myenergi zappi ev home charger available through a complimentary 12month subscription to zooms ev benefits bundle


 


available to new renewal and returning customers alike additional bundle rewards include discounts on parking charges special offers on ev accessories and discounts on public charging costs
1
 customers even have access to zooms team of ev experts who are on hand to offer indepth guidance and support via a dedicated helpline


 


steven williams head of ev strategy at direct line commented switching to electric is a big change for many motorists so were doing all we can to empower drivers that are looking to make the change to an ev


 


thanks to our partnership with zoom everyone who insures their ev with direct line benefits from a whole host of offers and discounts helping customers to make savings every year its just another way that were making the sometimes daunting thought of going electric easy for uk motorists


 


jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi added as the transition to electrification continues to accelerate more drivers than ever are making the switch to more sustainable travel by offering zappi to direct line customers through the zoom ev benefits bundle were working hard to ensure that drivers have access to a futureproof domestic charging solution with over 200 off the original price saving customers 15
2


 


in the future well be adding additional myenergi products to the deal giving customers easy access to our stateoftheart ecosystem of home energy tech from our eddi power diverter to our libbi smart home battery each model helps you cut carbon reduce reliance on mains supply and take complete control of energy consumption


 


direct line offer insurance for electric and hybrid cars with electric battery and charging cables covered following an insured incident every customer benefits from a free 12month subscription to zoom making it easier and cheaper than ever to switch to electric


 


greg fairbotham founder and ceo of zoom ev concluded were on a mission to enable the flexible use of electric vehicles provide the best ev benefits to drivers and reduce co2 emissions by making electric easy weve helped to save the equivalent of more than 1450 tonnes of co2  proudly playing our part in making a big difference


 


for more information about electric car insurance from direct line visit 
wwwdirectlinecomcarcoverelectric
 specifics of the ev benefits bundle can be found at 
wwwzoomevcomevbenefits



1
 bundle is subject to change


2
 savings calculated for 7kw tethered and untethered model alongside installation charges
