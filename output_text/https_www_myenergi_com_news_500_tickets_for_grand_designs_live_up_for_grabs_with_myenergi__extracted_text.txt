500 tickets for grand designs live up for grabs with myenergi
 


 








 










myenergi is giving away 500 tickets for this years grand designs live show at excel london from friday 30 april sunday 8 may


launched to celebrate the companys sponsorship of the 
green living live zone
 its a mustwin prize for anyone embarking on a home build renovation or improvement project


to be in with a chance of winning simply like myenergi on 
facebook
 or 
instagram
 and comment on the prize post tagging the person youd most like to take with you 250 lucky fans will take away a pair of tickets granting full entry to the exhibition and all breakout events for those who arent lucky enough to win myenergi will also unveil an exclusive 2 for 1 purchase code via social media ahead of the show




a celebration of sustainable building products planetfriendly construction materials renewable energy generation technologies innovative furnishings and electric car charging equipment the green living live zone at grand designs live will showcase some of the worlds most progressive brands theres also a
 live theatre stage
 and advice hub devoted to issues surrounding sustainability as well as expert panel discussions and free project advice


alongside sponsoring the zone and participating in panel discussions myenergi will also be exhibiting on stand q6a of the main exhibition the green team will showcase the companys latest range of ecosmart home energy technologies including its 
zappi
 electric vehicle charger 
eddi
 power diverter and 
harvi
 energy harvesting sensor whats more kevin mccloud host of 
channel 4s grand designs
 is set to make a meet and greet appearance


jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented as smart home technology becomes evermore commonplace across the uk selfbuilders renovators and property improvers are looking towards the latest innovations to make their projects both energy efficient and future proof as a true pioneer of planetfriendly technologies we were keen to showcase our latest products at grand designs live and sponsor the green living live zone


as well as meeting visitors our team will be on hand to discuss the value of renewable energy selfgeneration solutions such as solar pv and domestic wind turbines the most effective options and how our products can work alongside these technologies to provide complete zeroemissions energy independence


you can join myenergi at grand designs live the green team will be on stand 
q6a
