description


complete with a midlayer which is waterproof breathable and windproof the jacket is made using environmentally friendly materials


the manufacturer works with certified partners perpetual topgreen and repreve

to create recycled fibres from postconsumer plastic bottles in collaboration these partners allow the manufacturer to calculate the number of 1 litre bottles that would have been sent to landfill based on the size of a single size l garment


this product has been manufactured using 19 postconsumer pet plastic bottles the process to transform these bottles into the final product takes on 4 steps


the postconsumer plastic bottles are crushed down and shredded into flakes

shredded flakes from the bottles are processed and turned into fibres

fibres are processed into recycled yarn woven or knitted fabric

fabric is used to create result genuine recycled garments

all of the genuine recycled products are approved to the global recycled standard
