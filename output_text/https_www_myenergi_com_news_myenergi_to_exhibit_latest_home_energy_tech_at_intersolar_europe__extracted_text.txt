myenergi to exhibit latest home energy tech at intersolar europe
 


 








 










next week were heading over to intersolar europe to showcase our ecosmart range of home energy tech taking place at the messe münchen exhibition centre between 1416 june the event is widely regarded as the worlds most important trade show for the solar industry


 


from the zappi ev charger and eddi automatic power controller to the harvi energy harvesting sensor and allnew libbi smart battery visitors can get up close and personal with some of myenergis most innovative solar tech setting the standards in ecosmart innovation each product can operate independently or together as part of an integrated ecosystem to give users complete energy independence


 


throughout the show the team will be available to discuss the latest industry trends and developments alongside how solarcompatible technology is helping to make the connected smart home of tomorrow a reality today


 


jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented as europes largest solar show intersolar europe is a major event for the entire industry exhibiting provides the perfect opportunity to meet with thousands of visitors and talk to them about our latest ecosmart product range


 


while were best known for our zappi ev charger we also manufacture a much wider range of green energy solutions to help decarbonise homes as the smart home of tomorrow becomes a reality innovative tech like ours will prove evermore commonplace


 


for more information or to book your tickets visit wwwintersolardetickets to find out more about our latest range of ecosmart home energy tech head over to the intersolar stand and meet the team
