myenergi announced as finalist in 2023 business champion awards
 


 








 










jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi has been shortlisted as a finalist in the 
entrepreneur of the year
 category at the 2023 
business champion awards



recognising passion tenacity and a drive to succeed the awards celebrate innovation inspiration and the leadership qualities of entrepreneurs behind some of the uks most exciting young businesses


jordan caught the judging panels eye by demonstrating not only her commitment to driving myenergis continued internationalisation but also accelerating the global transition to netzero


as a leader in 
ecosmart home energy technology
 were committed to creating a better future being recognised on a national level for our continued success is truly humbling as we continue our mission to help homeowners all over the world embrace sustainability


co founder and ceo of myenergi lee sutton with cofounder and cmo jordan brompton


jordan commented


its a real privilege to be named as a finalist at this years business champion awards despite it being my name on the shortlist the award really is testament to the hard work of the entire team


when we founded the business our vision was simple  create a positive impact push the boundaries of possibility and change the world through pioneering green technology we started with nothing except for a few good ideas and a serious passion for change were looking forward to continuing to make our mark in the green energy sector for years to come


for more information about the business champion awards visit 
httpsbusinesschampionawardscouk2023finalists



the winners will be announced at a blacktie awards dinner hosted at the east wintergarden canary wharf on march 22nd
