myenergi takes home top gong at the auto trader ncas
 


 








 










the myenergi zappi britains bestselling ecosmart ev charging device was crowned best home charger at last nights auto trader new car awards 15 june based on realworld driver feedback from more than 220000 uk car owners zappi was rated better than any of its rivals


 


with responses submitted through an online survey zappi scored highly across 16 criteria  from reliability and performance to appearance cost and overall user satisfaction respondents were impressed with zappis ecosmart solar compatibility as well as technical support received from the customer service team


 


reflecting the uks rapidly accelerating transition to electrification 2023 was the inaugural year for the fiercely competitive best home charger category  one of 23 awards presented during the evening


 


jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented having zappi recognised as best home charger shows that ev owners really appreciate the care and attention that we put into each and every zappi device  testament to our amazing team across the business who are dedicated to delivering amazing products for our customers


 


at myenergi were committed to changing the way people think about energy with a diverse range of technologies designed to improve sustainability and minimise mains consumption since launching our first generation zappi device weve seen hundreds of thousands of units installed all over the world however despite this rapid growth weve stayed true to our promises of excellent performance outstanding customer service and unmatched reliability


 


erin baker auto traders editorial director added its clear that ev owners love their myenergi chargers with users ranking it top in the inaugural auto trader home charger of the year award myenergi scored highest in overall satisfaction with owners praising the ease of installation and value myenergi home chargers offer congratulations myenergi


 


a full list of winners from the 2023 auto trader new car awards can be found here 
wwwautotradercouknewcarawards
