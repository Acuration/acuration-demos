ct clamp small model


zappi  eddi are supplied with one wired ct terminal as standard


the 22kw 3phase zappi comes standard with 3 cts


one or 3 in case of a 3phase inverter additional ct clamp is needed if you want to see the real time output of your solarwind system on the products lcd display


this is a smaller version of the standard ct clamp you can use this ct to measure up to 40a moreover this is not a clipon model so you have to detach the relevant cable to put it through the ct this is only for the advanced electrician 


with this clamp terminal zappi can take measurements on the phase cables of the main fuse for its protection the consumption of the house the surplus of your own generation but also for the yield of your energy system solar panels or wind turbine


with the 1phase zappi and eddi 1 ct is supplied as standard with the 3phase zappi 3 ct clamps are supplied


with an additional ct you can also display the yield of your solar panels and read this on the display it is not necessary for the correct operation to also place a ct on the inverter this is only necessary to get a complete overview on the display and via the hub in the app of all the information about where the power is coming from or going to


for the zappi v1 models if you want to do a 3phase measurement you must use 3 cts in combination with harvi  you can only connect 2 terminals wired in the zappi v1


for the zappi v2 models if you want to do a 3phase measurement you can connect 3 clamps directly wired andor connect 3 extra clamps wirelessly with a harvi
