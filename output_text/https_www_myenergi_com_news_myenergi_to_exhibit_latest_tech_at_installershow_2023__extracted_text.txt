myenergi to exhibit latest tech at installershow 2023
 


 








 










were all set to to exhibit our latest tech at this years installershow taking place at the nec birmingham between 2729 june the event will be the uks largest trade exhibition for heat water air and energy professionals


on a stand next to the myenergisponsored café the team will showcase our latest range of home energy technologies including the zappi ev charger eddi automatic power controller harvi energy harvesting sensor and allnew libbi smart battery designed and manufactured in the uk each product can operate independently or together as part of an integrated ecosystem to give users complete energy independence


throughout the show well also be running a series of expert workshops from getting to know the myenergi product portfolio and becoming an accredited installer to upselling and exploring the usps of the range each private session will be run by myenergis dedicated inhouse training team


jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented as a major event in the installer calendar exhibiting at installershow 2023 will provide an excellent opportunity to meet with thousands of visitors and talk to them about our latest ecosmart myenergi product range


while were best known for our zappi ev charger we also manufacture a much wider range of green energy solutions for savvy homeowners as the smart home of tomorrow becomes a reality innovative tech like ours will prove evermore commonplace this presents a massive opportunity for installers to become accredited and fill their order books accordingly


to book your tickets for installershow visit wwwinstaller2023visitorregbuzzshowwebsite for more information about myenergi visit wwwmyenergicom or head over to the stand and meet the team
