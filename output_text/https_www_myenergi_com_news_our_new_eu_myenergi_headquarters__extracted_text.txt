our new eu myenergi headquarters
 


 








 










were delighted to announce that were expanding our business with a new myenergi headquarters in europe


our company mission has always been for 
renewable energy
 to become 
the new normal
 for everyone this exciting opportunity allows us to create jobs explore new avenues and lead the way for a brighter more sustainable future


where will the new headquarters be


our new myenergi office will be in maastricht south of the netherlands and it will enable us to form even stronger relationships with our european family


our new eu headquarters will be our second location in mainland europe as we continue to meet the growing demand for our products 
renewable energy production
 in the uk reached a record high of 423 in april and its only set to increase from here


our cofounder 
jordan brompton
 says this is just the start people need to be excited by this sector we want to grow and develop taking people with us on our journey


we have huge confidence in the growth of the 
green energy
 sector and our teams ability to deliver groundbreaking products


the expansion of myenergi keeps us at the forefront of developing innovative and accessible technology as increasing numbers of households worldwide take steps towards a more sustainable way of life


our netherlands office will be based on the same model as our german hq  and our newest office will distribute products across all three countries in the benelux area


who are the benelux myenergi team




 


the new headquarters will be run by the fantastic marc zeguers one of our 
myenergi family



we are delighted to be working even more closely with marc says jordan his understanding and enthusiasm for the brand is second to none and we cant think of anyone better to help us extend our presence across europe


not only has marc sold myenergi products for the last two years but he has also delivered copies of 
sparki
 to children in hospitals to raise money for neonatal intensive care units


this partnership really means a lot to me says marc we were always super enthusiastic about the myenergi products and im thrilled to become a member of the team and to help spread the word


from maastricht the benelux area is ideal to cover and because weve already experienced the demand for the eco solutions of myenergi i am convinced well have a prosperous future together as business partners


marcs commitment to making the world a better place is at the heart of what we do here at myenergi his enthusiasm and spirit will lead the way for this new chapter of our journey


the myenergi journey


were also expanding our reach here in the uk myenergi will be moving into our new 
15500 sq ft flagship headquarters
 in the coming weeks the move will allow us to branch out our 
products
 and development and we cant wait to grow and keep moving forward


we will never stop striving to make our world a greener kinder place 
our myenergi journey
 has seen us grow from a small green tech company in binbrook to an international award winning team of industry leaders


weve written books filed patents and released innovative technology to thousands of people as part of our mission  to change the world through green technology


and our journey has only just begun
