myenergi academi supports national apprenticeship week 2023
 


 








 










following the opening of the 
myenergi academi
 last year were proud to support 
national apprenticeship week
 6th  12th february to highlight the benefits of apprenticeships and what they bring to local communities businesses and the wider economy


this years theme skills for life was selected to highlight how workbased training can help the future workforce build real life skills and knowledge our dedicated education hub based at our stateoftheart eco headquarters in stallingborough encourages workbased learning and ongoing development while allowing members of the academi to 
earn while they learn



our grimsbybased apprenticeship programmes start in september and are available across a range of departments depending on business needs from engineering and manufacturing to procurement and marketing as a myenergi apprentice 80 of the programme is spent working firsthand across a variety of projects while the remaining 20 is training to develop new and existing skills


commenting on the academi myenergi cofounder jordan brompton said


myenergi is growing at an exceptional rate and our academi allows us to identify the talented team members of tomorrow were enthusiastic about supporting the region in which we operate and our apprenticeship programmes not only enable us to bolster our workforce but also support the local area aiding its development and that of the renewables sector


the myenergi academi offers apprenticeships and internships through all areas of the business with plans to introduce postgraduate schemes in the future it is designed for school college and university leavers looking to kickstart their career or careerchangers who are looking to enter the greentech space
