myenergi  celebrating 5 years of tech innovation


 


were delighted to announce our fifth anniversary milestone since launching the business in 2016 weve expanded rapidly thanks to our unique range of ecosmart energy management products  including the worlds first renewable energycompatible ev charger 


 


we founded myenergi in 2016 as a partnership between two friends  electronics engineer lee sutton and marketing afficionado jordan brompton from our humble beginnings designing products and mailing them to customers via the local post office to operating a completely global production and distribution network were truly humbled to have become a worldwide market leader


 


 


this month were all set to open the doors to our brandnew flagship headquarters however planning is already underway on the second phase of development which will see the site expand by almost 50000 sq ft to house our extensive production operations


wed like to thank each and every myenergi customer for their support and encouragement over the past five years we wouldnt be here without you


happy birthday love sparki the alien


high five for myenergi anniversary milestone


 


myenergi the awardwinning designer and manufacturer of renewable energy products for ecosmart home management is celebrating its fifth anniversary milestone since its inception in 2016 the company has expanded rapidly launching an industrydefining range of ecosmart energy management products  including the worlds first renewable energycompatible 
ev charger
 


 


from its humble beginnings designing products manufacturing them by hand and mailing them to customers via the local post office to operating a stateoftheart production line and managing a truly global distribution network myenergi is renowned for driving the future of ecosmart home management 


 


in july the company will officially open the doors to its brandnew 15000 sq ft flagship headquarters in the uk planning is already underway on the second phase of development which will see the site expand by 50000 sq ft to house the companys extensive production operations


 


with subsidiaries in ireland benelux belgium the netherlands luxembourg  myenergi is targeting further international growth with a whole host of new products planned for the coming months the business is set to skyrocket 


 


jordan brompton cofounder and cmo at myenergi commented celebrating our fifth anniversary is a special milestone when we founded the business back in 2016 we started with nothing  except of course a few good ideas and a serious passion for change today we employ more than 200 team members and operate subsidiaries worldwide manufacturing some of the most innovative smart home devices ever brought to market 


 


we are proud to have more than 25000 connected customers via our myenergi app and recently surpassed 115000 units shipped a major landmark for our growing company weve taken risks overcome obstacles and learnt from our mistakes perseverance and a desire to be successful have seen us build a unique product range and a hugely talented team 


looking forward to many exciting years ahead especially in our state of the art new office and manufacturing facilities
