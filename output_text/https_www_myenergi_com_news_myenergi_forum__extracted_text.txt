the myenergi forum surpasses 1000 users
 


 








 










in difficult times such as these when most of us are selfisolating at home and contact between friends coworkers and family is minimal the importance of connection and collaboration is truly shown


with more and more people relying on digital communication we thought now is a perfect opportunity to talk about our myenergi forum and to thank each and every one of you who has contributed to our online community so far


our forum recently surpassed over 1000 users in less than four months if you would like to get involved join the online conversation and remain connected with your fellow ev users and ecowarriors find out everything you need to know below


what is the myenergi forum


what do you get when you add the fastpaced nature of technology to the increasing demand for renewable energy and the determination of ecoconscious enthusiasts to create a more sustainable future for our planet


you get the incredible industry in which we work where fantastic people who truly care about their carbon footprint work together towards one mission to save our home


it can be tricky to keep up with the foreverinnovating and advancing renewables industry especially with the likes of electric vehicle software changes and firmware updates this has of course been made even more difficult due to shops and stores closing as a result of the 
coronavirus
 however when youre a member of our 
myenergi forum
 you always have somewhere to turn to for help and guidance


created in november 2019 our forum is the goto place for our customers supporters and fans to meet discuss provide feedback and share ideas




we are so blessed to operate in an industry in which everyone is so open to collaborating and working together as one to build a brighter future therefore we decided that these fantastic ecowarriors 
ev drivers
 
renewable energy
 users and early adopters deserve somewhere they can connect and speak with one another remotely from the comfort of their own homes


tackling everything from 
teslas
 latest software updates to myenergi product installation guides industry news and technical difficulties  the myenergi forum is 
the 
goto resource for all things myenergi ev and renewable


why should i join the myenergi forum


whether youre a myenergi customer an ev driver or simply a renewable energy enthusiast there is something for everyone in our forum 


even if you do not currently own any of our products but are looking for somewhere to connect with likeminded individuals during the 
covid19 crisis
 we would welcome you with open digital arms to our forum


one of the most amazing things about 
myenergiinfo
 is that now that we have over 1000 members it has become selfsustaining what do we mean by that you ask due to the growing number of members forum users are now answering one anothers questions sometimes without our input at all


in such an inclusive and friendly industry such as ours why wouldnt you want to be a part of a community where you can get in touch with myenergi developers and installers directly and remotely while continuing to help solve and answer each others problems and queries


what topics are discussed in the myenergi forum


forum members can open up discussions of their own or participate in fellow members queries and topic suggestions recently conversations have stemmed around




installation guides on the zappi eddi and harvi


tesla
 updates


vw egolf charging modes


firmware updates on myenergi products


tips on reducing carbon footprint

renewable energy
 enthusiasts in our free online community simply click the link below
