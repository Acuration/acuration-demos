ct clamp


zappi  eddi are supplied with one wired ct terminal as standard


the 22kw 3phase zappi comes standard with 3 cts


an additional ct clamp is needed if you want to see the realtime output of your solarwind system on your products lcd display


in some cases additional ct terminals are required for example for 3phase installations


the ct terminals come with a 5 metre cable if a longer cable is required it can be extended with a cat5 ethernet cable or you can consider using the myenergi harvi to connect the ct wirelessly


delivery time 1 to 2 days
