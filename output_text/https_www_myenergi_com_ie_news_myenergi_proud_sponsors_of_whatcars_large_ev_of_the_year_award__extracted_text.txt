myenergi proud sponsors of whatcars large ev of the year award
 


 








 










myenergi are extremely honoured and proud to sponsor the large ev category of the whatcar car of the year awards 2020


as market leaders in electric vehicle charging and renewable energy we could not think of a better award to sponsor and support


what are the whatcar car of the year awards 2020


the awards also known as the uks most prestigious car awards ceremony takes place  in london on 14th january 2020 


crowning the very best in the automotive industry the 
whatcar car of the year awards
 are given to the cars that set the highest standards in their class each year


what is the large ev of the year category


large ev electric vehicle is an extremely prestigious accolade within the whatcar car of the year awards 2020 particularly given the increasing popularity and focus on electric cars right now


this category celebrates the very best large electric vehicle of the last 12 months


hybrid and 
electric cars
 have become a more viable option for many motorists due to their efficiency fuel economy and vastly reduced emissions compared to petrol and diesel vehicles


the popularity and impressive innovation of evs was underlined in last years awards when the kia eniro walked away with the highly sought after 
car of the year 2019
 and 
best electric car 2019
 awards


why did we pick this award


not only are many of our wonderful customers ev owners but most of our team are too 


our mission is to change the world through green technology how do we do this by designing manufacturing and installing sustainable products that help people make the most of renewable selfconsumed green energy


zappi
 one of our bestselling products is actually an ecosmart ev charger 
zappi
 makes owning an electric vehicle even more environmentally friendly by unlocking the opportunity to selfconsume green 
renewable energy
 every time you charge your vehicle


so who better to sponsor the ev category of one of the most distinguished and reputable automotive awards than us


good luck to all nominees


wed like to take this opportunity to wish all of the motor manufacturers in the whatcar car of the year awards 2020 the very best of luck and to congratulate each and every one of you for your efforts in driving innovation and the quest for a renewable future
