myenergi to discuss future of smart charging at solar and storage live
 


 








 










later this month 1719 october were heading to the nec in birmingham for solar and storage live 2023 the uks leading exhibition for renewable energy will welcome more than 450 exhibitors 300 speakers and tens of thousands of visitors


 


as part of a jampacked schedule well be delivering three keynote presentations exploring myenergis connected home energy ecosystem on day one 17 october our senior product manager andy ireland will be on stage in the largescale utility theatre at 220pm explaining the potential of power diverters in 
eddi  the solar superhero



 


on day two head over to the storage and batteries theatre at 11am to hear dr chris horne our chief technical officer explore 
life with libbi  getting to energy independence
 later in the day 1pm youll find our field support manager jonno smith hosting a talk titled 
unveiling myenergis product champion
  youre not going to want to miss it


 


our head of corporate communications tom callow will also be taking to the stage for a panel discussion titled 
on the plus side smartcharging and the ev consumer
 the conversation will take place in the chargev theatre on 17 october at 250pm


 


the green team will also be on hand throughout the threeday event to showcase the latest home energy technology the myenergi stand g12 will be located next to the café


 


jordan brompton our cofounder and cmo commented solar and storage live is a major event in the home energy industry calendar since last years show weve launched our allnew libbi smart battery and grown our global customer base significantly so were looking forward to sharing our insights with delegates and other exhibitors alike


 


were looking forward to discussing all things smart charging at the on the plus side panel discussion with tom but each of our keynote presentations are a mustattend too  experts across the myenergi team share knowledge about unlocking the true potential of renewables and empower consumers to embrace the energy system of tomorrow


 


for more information about solar and storage live or to book your tickets for this years event visit 
httpswwwterrapinncomexhibitionsolarstorageliveindexstm
 if youd like to speak to us in person our green team will be located just to the left of the entrance
