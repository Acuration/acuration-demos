myenergi announced as global innovation partner for fully charged live
 


 








 










were delighted to announce that myenergi has 
teamed up with fully charged live
 as global innovation partner for 2023


the agreement will see us travel the world  from australia and canada to america and amsterdam  for each international instalment of the worlds leading electric vehicle and home energy show


since the very first 
fully charged live
 show in 2018 weve been a regular exhibitor travelling across europe and north america to showcase our latest product innovations with the show set to expand its world tour in 2023 we were keen to put pen to paper as the events first official partner


the show schedule has already been confirmed with the myenergi team already preparing for the first event in australia next month

jordan brompton cofounder of myenergi at fully charged live


jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi explains


as one of the worlds fastestgrowing ecosmart tech companies our products are used by homeowners all over the globe to that end it makes absolute sense to partner with fully charged live on a global basis for all six events this year we have always admired what the team is aiming to do with a shared ambition to support the switch to renewable energy
 electric vehicles
 and 
ecosmart homes



ceo of fully charged live dan caesar adds


we have worked closely with myenergi over the past six years and we are delighted to further strengthen our relationship with their confirmation as fully charged lives first global partner their commitment to support all six of our live shows in 2023 is testament to the success of the events and also the innovative vision of their organisation


with plans to take its business into further global markets we know fully charged live offers myenergi the perfect platform to reach thousands of new potential customers
