iconic sponsorship deal with grimsby town fc home  news  myenergi announces iconic sponsorship deal with grimsby town fc myenergi pioneer of the worlds first ecosmart ev charger is delighted to be partnering with grimsby town fc as the official club shirt sponsor for the 202223 season the announcement comes just days after the clubs promotion into the football league as part of the agreement the myenergi logo will feature across the mariners first team academy and womens team kit myenergi will also be a key partner to the club in community and culturefocussed projects driving social change to build a stronger community with a more positive cultural perception headquartered in stallingborough but with manufacturing facilities in the heart of grimsby myenergi is one of the regions fastestgrowing companies and largest employers best known for zappi electric vehicle charger the business is putting the town on the map as a national hub of renewable technology innovation committed to its grimsby roots myenergi is expanding at a rapid pace the business has recently launched its 2022 recruitment drive with countless roles available for local residents across a wide range of disciplines and departments  from engineering and production to procurement software sales and technical services jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented when the opportunity came up to sponsor grimsby town fc we jumped at the chance the club is a massive part of the local community and while having a new sponsor is a big change after 18 years we hope we can do the fans justice we want to celebrate the towns central role in the renewable energy revolution as proud grimbarians and passionate mariners fans were excited to kickoff the new partnership and look forward to seeing the myenergi logo emblazoned across the grimsby town fc kit next season mariners fans will have seen iconic grimsby landmarks lit up in green over the past few days as a trailblazer in sustainability and renewable technology myenergi is committed to making grimsby europes green town jason stockwood the chair of grimsby town fc added its a real pleasure to welcome myenergi as the official club shirt sponsor of grimsby town fc the agreement is an important step forward in the clubs bright future and were delighted that this will be with such a progressive local business the myenergi team are
