myenergi pin gin goes green with ecosmart ev charging points home  blog  pin gin goes green with ecosmart ev charging points bottomley distillers renowned for its artisan pin gin spirit range has installed two stateoftheart electric vehicle charging points at its new headquarters in louth lincolnshire committed to sustainability the company prides itself on ethical sourcing and environmentallyfriendly operation the zappi chargers manufactured by fellow lincolnshire business myenergi will provide lowcarbon vehicle charging for guests touring the visitor centre the only device on the market capable of utilising 100 green energy surplus electricity generated by the distillerys solar panels will be redirected to power visitors vehicles installing the zappi chargers is the latest in a long line of sustainability commitments from bottomley distillers with carbon reduction at the heart of operations the company has committed to achieving a net zero environmental impact myenergi providing sustainable electric car charging solutions to family run bottomley distillery in the heart of the lincolnshire wolds amy conyard cofounder of bottomley distillers commented earlier this year we relocated to a site fourteen times the size of our previous distillery the new space is perfect for our rapid expansion plans and will house our brand new 5000 litre still while the new distillery is almost operational were also in the process of creating a visitor centre which is set to open later this year installing the zappi chargers will allow guests to charge their cars when they come to visit as well as looking great and providing a huge variety of charging modes theyre the most sustainable charging point on the market  a hugely important element for us premium pink pin gin 70cl jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi added lincolnshire is home to some of britains most exciting upandcoming brands like myenergi bottomley distillers and the pin gin brand is growing rapidly and were excited to watch the companys continued journey supplying a pair of zappi chargers will not only provide energy independence but also futureproof the business working in harmony with the facilitys pv panels our technology will charge evs with completely free 100 green energy  the perfect circular economy as the uk transitions towards electrification myenergi is renowned for designing the worlds first solar compatible ev charger  zappi alongside operating as standard for electric car charging it has optional charging modes to utilise 100 green energy generated from your solar pv panels with a whole host of carefullydesigned features to give you complete control of your charging experience it can be paired with the myenergi app to utilise economy tariffs deploy the boost function and provide insightful monitoring data worlds first solar compatible ev charger zappi since merging with next frontier brands in september 2020 bottomley distillers has continued its dedication to positively impacting the local visitor economy local area investment and job creation next frontier brands is global provider of fastmoving consumer goods dedicated to bringing premier beverage and hempbased wellness brands to consumers globally the company is headquartered in boulder colorado with additional offices in london and new zealand share story related
