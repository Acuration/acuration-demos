energy to reduce co2 emissions it is important to switch from fossil fuels to cleaner green energy by using energy from renewable sources we can counteract climate change there are roughly 5 types of renewable energy we will explain them all below 1 wind energy wind energy is generated by wind turbines these turbines convert the power of the wind into usable energy it is a clean and sustainable form of energy it is one of the most important types of renewable energy and is often used in the netherlands it is also possible to install a wind turbine at home however these often do not produce enough energy to power your entire home 2 solar energy it is estimated that around 15 million households in the netherlands use solar energy by means of their own solar panels of all the types of renewable energy this form is the easiest to generate at home solar panels make use of sunlight and convert it into usable energy that you can use at home do you want to make maximum use of the energy you generate this is possible with our innovative zappi this smart system uses your selfgenerated solar energy to charge your electric car zappi consists of three modes between which it can easily switch to make the most efficient and effective use of your energy production 3 geothermal energy geothermal energy literally means geothermal energy it uses the heat from the earth to generate energy using the heat from the ground you can heat and cool your home with a special geothermal heat pump 4 hydroelectricity when we talk about types of renewable energy hydroelectricity is not to be missed it is electricity generated by hydropower and accounts for no less than 71 per cent of renewable energy worldwide 5 biomass biomass extracts energy from organic sources such as agricultural crops kitchen and garden waste and manure these substances are fermented or gasified into biofuel when this biofuel is subsequently burned energy is released learn more about these types of renewable energy
