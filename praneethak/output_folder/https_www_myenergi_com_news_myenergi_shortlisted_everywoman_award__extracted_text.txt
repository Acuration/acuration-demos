national technology awards home  awards  myenergi shortlisted in national technology awards jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of ecosmart tech pioneer myenergi has been announced as a finalist in the entrepreneur category at this years fdm everywoman in technology awards aiming to shine a spotlight on the sectors most exceptional talent the awards celebrate women at every stage of their career journey recognising their ambition innovative thinking and commitment to making a positive impact each finalist has been chosen for their relentless drive to inspire others a true industry disruptor the judges were impressed by jordans leading role in the international growth of myenergi  best known for developing our zappi device the worlds first ecosmart ev charger in just over 5 years we have grown to staff numbers in excess of 300 with more than 3000 approved installers and subsidiaries worldwide during the past year alone our profitability has increased by more than 2000 myenergis new stateoftheart headquarters in north east lincolnshire jordan brompton cofounder and cmo at myenergi commented cofounder  chief marketing officer jordan brompton when we founded myenergi the vision was simple  create a positive impact push the boundaries of possibility and change the world through pioneering green technology as society transitions away from fossil fuels and towards the zerocarbon world of tomorrow we want to provide the technologies to make ecosmart home management simple and achievable our innovative products are built on a commitment to circularity and a vision of sustainability for generations to come being shortlisted in the fdm everywoman in technology awards is a real honour im looking forward to telling my story sharing myenergis vision and using the opportunity as a platform to inspire more women to take up roles in the tech industry a representative at the everywoman in technology awards added jordan is a true leader in the sector and is committed to leading the uks transition to electrification her journey story passion and enthusiasm has built a global following of ecosavvy consumers who are each playing their part in the renewable
