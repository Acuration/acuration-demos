2022 ideal home show home  car charging  myenergi to attend the 2022 ideal home show myenergi are kicking off their 2022 events calendar by exhibiting its nextgeneration product range at this years ideal home show taking place at the london olympia between 1127 march the event brings together hundreds of exhibitors and thousands of visitors to explore the latest innovative products and ontrend solutions for your home on stand g120 myenergi will showcase its latest range of ecosmart home energy technologies including its zappi electric vehicle charger eddi automatic power controller and harvi energy harvesting sensor designed and manufactured in the uk each product gives users the power to control their homes flow of energy alongside introducing its nextgeneration product portfolio the green team will be available to discuss the governments recent ev charging legislation announcement and what this means for property developers and homeowners alike set to come into force later this year the bill will require all new build homes and commercial offices to feature smart ev charging technology coming as part of the governments roadmap to electrifying transport and its 2030 ban on the sale of all new petrol and diesel cars the legislation aims to further boost the uks vehicle charging infrastructure with 25700 public charge points already operational nationwide and countless more home ev chargers installed this latest move will see the network expand exponentially  an essential step in supporting the drivers of tomorrow jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented as one of the worlds longestrunning exhibitions exhibiting at the ideal home show will provide an excellent opportunity to meet with thousands of visitors and talk to them about how myenergi tech can give them complete energy independence while were best known for our zappi ev charging device we manufacture a comprehensive range of products for savvy homeowners as the smart home of
