intelligent battery software to coordinate energy production smart control systems ensure that the battery is not overcharging or discharging ineffectively energy is released from the battery storage system during peak times which keeps costs down and electricity flowing find out more about libbi discover more about libbi why should i have a battery storage system there are a multitude of different reasons why you should have a battery storage system one of which is the savings you could see on your energy bills you can also be sure that youre utilising any of the spare renewable demand is high your household can avoid relying on the grid altogether especially with libbis smart controls which communicate with grid controllers so not only will you be seeing financial and
