blog  grand designs live event myenergi set to exhibit nextgen tech at grand designs live event in birmingham myenergi pioneer of the worlds first ecosmart ev charging device is set to exhibit its nextgeneration product range at grand designs live taking place at the nec in birmingham 610 october the event brings together hundreds of exhibitors and thousands of visitors to explore the latest selfbuild and home renovation solutions grand designs live 2021 on eyecatching stand b69 at grand designs live myenergi will showcase its latest range of ecosmart home energy technologies including its zappi electric vehicle charger eddi solar power diverter and harvi energy harvesting sensor designed and manufactured in the uk each product gives users the power to control their homes flow of energy myenergi stand at b69 alongside introducing its nextgeneration product portfolio the greenteam will be available to discuss the governments recent ev charging legislation announcement and what this means for property developers and homeowners alike set to come into force next year the bill will require all new build homes and commercial offices to feature smart ev charging technology coming as part of the governments roadmap to electrifying transport and its 2030 ban on the sale of all new petrol and diesel cars the legislation aims to further boost the uks vehicle charging infrastructure with 25700 public charge points already operational nationwide and countless more home ev chargers installed this latest move will see the network expand exponentially  an essential step in supporting the drivers of tomorrow jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented as one of the largest and most popular home events of its kind anywhere in europe exhibiting at grand designs live will provide an excellent opportunity to meet with thousands of visitors and talk to them about how myenergi tech can give them complete energy independence while were best known for our zappi ev charging device we manufacture a comprehensive range of products for savvy homeowners as the smart home of tomorrow becomes a reality innovative tech like ours will prove evermore commonplace
