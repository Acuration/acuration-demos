myenergi myenergi partners with santander to decarbonise uk homes home  blog  myenergi partners with santander to decarbonise uk homes were pleased to have announced a strategic partnership with santander the agreement which will see myenergis products promoted at preferential rates through the santander website and digital banking app offers a simple solution to help uk account holders decarbonise their homes from the eddi solar diverter and zappi ev charger to the allnew libbi home battery system santanders seven million online customers will be given immediate digital access to the entire myenergi ecosystem through this simple digital journey users can purchase the latest tech arrange delivery and organise installation  simple sustainable and hasslefree jordan brompton cofounder and cmo of myenergi commented at myenergi weve developed a comprehensive smart home energy ecosystem that helps users to save money save energy and lower their carbon footprint whether you already have renewable microgeneration technology installed or not such as pv panels or air source heat pumps our products can help you to decarbonise your home and reduce reliance on mains supply as well as enabling homeowners to become more energy efficient our ambition is to make ecosmart tech available and affordable for everyone by partnering with santander we aim to provide millions of account holders nationwide with exclusive deals and preferential prices  a significant step forward tracie pearce chief customer officer at santander homes said from speaking to our customers a big priority is making their homes more energy efficient to lower monthly household bills and this partnership with myenergi gives them the opportunity to do exactly that customers can view the my home manager feature on the santander mobile app a unique onestop home management service and browse a range of myenergi products that can improve the energy consumption of a home conveniently customers can also arrange purchase delivery and installation of all myenergi products for added ease and convenience the strategic partnership offers preferential rates on myenergis complete product range
