SunEarth Black Chrome (EC Series) Collectors Discontinued

Monday, January 4, 2021

After decades of being one of the most popular flat plate collector choices on the market, SunEarth has discontinued the black chrome (EC) series Empire collectors. The choice to discontinue the collector was made due to availability problems with the black chrome coating, along with the popularity of the newer and better performing ThermoRay (TRB) panels.

All warranties on existing panels will continue to be serviced.

The current line of SunEarth panels now includes the Selective Paint (EP) for standard efficiency and warmer climates, and the ThermoRay (TRB) for high efficiency in colder climates.

Sun Source Energy Products still has a small stock of EC-32 and EC-40 panels, and these are available at attractive pricing. Please contact us for availability and current pricing. Don't wait, once these are sold there will be no more!
