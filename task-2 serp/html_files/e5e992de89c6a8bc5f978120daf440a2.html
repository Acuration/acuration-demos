Who We Are

SunSource Energy provides distributed energy solutions for commercial and industrial customers in India with a presence across South-East Asia. SunSource develops, engineers, procures, constructs, and operates solar power projects to help institutional, industrial, commercial and non-profit sector energy users utilize solar energy more intelligently and efficiently while reducing their cost of energy.

Who We Are

SunSource Energy provides distributed energy solutions for commercial and industrial customers in India with a presence across South-East Asia. SunSource develops, engineers, procures, constructs, and operates solar power projects to help institutional, industrial, commercial and non-profit sector energy users utilize solar energy more intelligently and efficiently while reducing their cost of energy.

SunSource Energy is part
of SHV Energy

SHV Energy is a leading global distributor of off-grid energy with 30 million customers across 4 continents. It is a wholly owned subsidiary of SHV, a global 125-year-old family business with a portfolio of companies in over 60 countries. SunSource Energy is proud to bring the stability, global view and long-term approach of SHV to India and other regions of focus.

Our Story

SunSource Energy was born with a vision of simplifying the clean energy of corporates, industries and institutions. Among the first wave of solar companies in India when it started, SunSource Energy has today established itself as a strong player in Indias hyper-competitive solar space and has supported 100+ customers with their energy transition.

Start of SunSource Energy

SunSource Energy was founded in 2010, when two solar engineers, Kushagra Nandan and Adarsh Das, joined hands and decided to come back to India after spending a decade in the US solar market. With a long-term vision of becoming a solar IPP, SunSource Energy started its operations as a solar engineering services provider.

Diverse Energy, Diverse Workplace

We invest in a workforce as diverse as the range of projects we deliver. For us, diversity is not only the right thing, its a vital thing in terms of the skills, experiences and viewpoints needed to drive ourselves and our customers to a sustainable energy future.
