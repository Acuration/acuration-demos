ABOUT SUNSOURCE:

SunSource Energy was born with a dream – of the entire world running on clean energy. Thus, the company envisioned building solar and clean energy assets around the world, in line with global standards. It started in 2010 in a living room office in Gurgaon, Delhi NCR. The company was among the first wave of solar companies in India at that point of time, and have since established ourselves as a strong player in India’s hyper-competitive solar space. SunSource Energy develops, engineers, procures, constructs, and operates solar power plants and projects, to help clients in institutional, industrial, commercial and non-profit sectors use solar energy more intelligently and efficiently.

  • Turnkey EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Services)
  • EPC and Advisory Services
  • Design Engineering
  • Project Site Diagnostics/Feasibility Assessment
