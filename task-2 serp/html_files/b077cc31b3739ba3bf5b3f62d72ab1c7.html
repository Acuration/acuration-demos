Joining Forces Announcement

The P.S.I. Fluid Power management team is pleased to announce that P.S.I. has joined forces with SunSource, based in Addison, IL. SunSource specializes in Products, Service & Repair for Industrial and Mobile Fluid Power applications, Fluid Process & Flow Control, Piping, Tubing & Fittings, Hydraulics & Motion Control, and Mining Products.
The partnership between the two companies will benefit P.S.I. Fluid Power, SunSource and all of our associates, supplier partners, and customers. P.S.I. will become a core entity of the SunSource Fluid Power business unit. This SunSource group specializes in motion control products and systems for industrial and mobile OEM customers, including service and repair offerings. Our continued drive for growth with these products in our geographical region will remain our primary focus.

Why are we joining forces?
SunSource brings strength to ensure our continued prosperity and growth.
SunSource offers additional capital to invest in resources required to grow with our customers and provide world class customer service with business systems, technical sales and service, local inventory and customer systems integration.
P.S.I. Fluid Power and SunSource share a common vision and values in business ethics, team recognition and retention, customer focus, and long-term business relationships with our supplier partners. Our steadfast commitment to our supplier partners, as well as our customers remains the same.

What are the benefits?
While there are many benefits to talk about in the future, specifically, we will have the ability to add more resources to continue to make future improvements in:
• IT/business systems 
• Customer Service and support
• In-house and on-site service and repair
• Customer training programs for market trends, product enhancements and safety
• Focused customer inventory

What is not changing?
• Management team – The P.S.I. management team remains in place to manage our business
• P.S.I. Fluid Power name
• Sales representatives and customer service personnel
• Product and service capabilities
• 24/7 customer support

If you have questions, please reach out to your P.S.I. sales or customer service representative. Or feel free to call any member of the P.S.I. Management team listed below. We could not be more pleased that SunSource has chosen P.S.I. to become part of the SunSource family. These are exciting times for P.S.I. Fluid Power and all of our associates as we strengthen the company to continue serving our customers as we have for the past 52 years.

For more information on SunSource please visit www.sun-source.com
