Sun Source Energy is a solar company based in Las Vegas, Nevada, founded in 2017. The company pairs industry leading solar products with a seamless solar installation process, and provides a 25-year performance warranty. Sun Source Energy handles all aspects of the solar installation process, including residential solar permitting, and seeks to help customers go solar with $0 down financing.

The Good

  • Top-Notch Solar Products
  • Seamless Installation Process
  • $0 Down Financing
  • 25-Year Warranty
  • Solar Monitoring

Top-Notch Solar Products

As a solar installation contractor, Sun Source Energy has partnerships with leading solar technology companies, such as Aptos Solar and SolarEdge. Through key partnerships, Sun Source Energy can provide reliable and efficient solar panel systems, including panels, inverters, and batteries.

Seamless Installation Process

Sun Source Energy has an efficient and seamless solar installation process. Before installing a solar system on your roof, Sun Source Energy will take care of all pre-installation details and processes, including residential permitting. The permitting process includes solar system design, engineering, code compliance, and zoning — Sun Source Energy will cover all additional costs that may arise during this process.

In addition, the company will ensure that your solar system meets state and local regulations, and acquire all necessary permits.

Once all permitting requirements are met, Sun Source Energy will install your solar system, including industry-leading products from the companies within its network. 

Throughout the solar installation process, Sun Source Energy provides personalized customer support.

$0 Down Financing

Going solar can be expensive. For this reason, many solar companies offer financing options that can make solar a reality for more customers. Sun Source Energy offers $0 down financing and works with a variety of lenders to customize the financing process for each customer.

25-Year Warranty

Sun Source Energy provides a 25-year performance warranty on its panels, ensuring that your investment is protected against damage or issues in the future.

Solar Monitoring

Sun Source Energy offers support and service beyond initial solar installation. Hand-in-hand with its 25-year performance warranty, the company provides solar system monitoring to keep an eye on your system’s performance. Sun Source Energy provides monitoring services via the SolarEdge monitoring app, which allows you to monitor and optimize your solar system.

The Bad

  • Limited Service Area

Limited Service Area

Sun Source Energy’s solar installation services are only available to Las Vegas, Nevada residents. Unfortunately, if you don’t live in this area, Sun Source Energy’s service will not be available to you.

It is not uncommon for solar companies to service limited areas. However, some solar companies do serve a larger area, including various states. You may also be able to discover a local solar company in your area.

gavel

The Bottom Line

For residents of Las Vegas, Sun Source Energy could be a great option for your solar installation. The company works with a variety of leading solar tech companies, ensuring that all solar equipment installed on your roof is high-quality, resulting in a solar system that will perform optimally and efficiently.

To make solar a reality for more customers, Sun Source Energy offers $0 down financing through a variety of lenders.

Every Sun Source Energy solar installation includes a 25-year warranty and solar monitoring services administered by the SolarEdge monitoring app. If you have any questions or concerns at any point during the solar installation process, Sun Source Energy provides customer support to answer your questions and address any concerns.
