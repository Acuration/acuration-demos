Awards

Over the years Suzlon and its late founder Tulsi Tanti have been bestowed with numerous awards and recognised for innovation and entrepreneurship.

These testaments to Suzlon’s consistent efforts to redefine the renewable energy sector and powering a greener tomorrow are listed below according to the category:

Notable Awards Received by Suzlon

GWEC felicitates Shri Tulsi Tanti for their lifetime contribution to Renewable Energy Industry Awards 2023-24

Energy Company of the Year 2023 (Renewables)
The award recognizes the most significant contributions to the development and promotion of the renewable energy industry in the country.

The Economic Times Sustainable Organizations 2023
Outstanding organizations that have demonstrated a remarkable commitment to integrated planning, development, and monitoring of sustainability in order to achieve sustainable development goals.

CBIP Award 2022 for 'The Best Contribution in Wind Energy' by the Central Board of Irrigation and Power, presented to Suzlon Energy Limited on 3rd March 2023.

Indian Wind Energy Forum 2023, Best OMS Service Provider, conferred to Suzlon Global Services Limited by WindInsider on 12th April 2023.

Golden Peacock National Training Award, in the Power (Renewable) Sector for 2023, conferred by Institute of Directors (IOD) India, to Suzlon Global Learning & Development (GLD) on 15th March 2023.
