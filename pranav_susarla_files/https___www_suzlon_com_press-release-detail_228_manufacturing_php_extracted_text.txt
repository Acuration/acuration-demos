July 16, 2010
 Suzlon wins 19.2 MW repeat order from the Baidyanath Group
Second order to Suzlon from Sidhayu Aryuvedic Research Foundation, a Baidyanath group companyNew order to take the Baidyanath group wind portfolio to 26.7 MWPune: Suzlon Energy Limited (SEL), the worlds third leading* and Indias largest windturbine manufacturer, has won a repeat order from Siddhayu Ayurvedic ResearchFoundation Private Limited  a Baidyanath group company. Suzlon will set up, operate andmaintain the 19.2 MW project, which will be installed in the Dhule and Satara districts in thestate of Maharashtra. The project comprises of three units of Suzlons S82  1.5 MW, andseven units of Suzlons S88  2.1 MW wind turbine generators (WTG).The Baidyanath Group is a leading name in the field of Ayurvedic Medicines with a standingof more than 90 years. Siddhayu Ayurvedic Research Foundation Private Limited, aBaidyanath group company, is a leading manufacturer of private label, custom and preformulatedAyurvedic Medicines, as well as organic and herbal products for reputeddomestic & international pharmaceutical companies. The 19.2 MW project is Sindhayussecond order with Suzlon and will take their wind portfolio to 26.7 MW.The project will be set up in two phases and shall be completed by March 2011. The Groupplans to register the project with UNFCCC under the Clean Development Mechanism (CDM).Mr. Pranav Sharma  Director, Siddhayu Ayurvedic Research Foundation PrivateLimited said: We are a group with a very strong drive towards sustainability and thisrepeat order speaks for our commitment towards a greener tomorrow. Our association withSuzlon is strengthened with this new order. We count on Suzlons collaborative approachand capabilities to continue our journey on the green path.Mr. Ashok DSa, President  South Asia and Middle East - Suzlon Energy Limited,said: This order underlines Suzlons unique value proposition to the industry  offeringexpertise across the value chain from concept to commissioning and operation of windprojects. We look forward to the delivery of yet another successful project that reinforcesthe commitment to renewable energy investments by the Baidyanath Group.
