September 11, 2008
 Suzlon secures orders in Romania, Portugal
Pune: Suzlon Energy A/S (SWEAS), the Europe and Latin America arm of SuzlonEnergy Limited, secured a major contract with Martifer for the delivery of 29 S88  2.1MW turbines, totaling a capacity of 60.9 MW, to projects in Romania. This orderrepresents Suzlons first wind turbine order in Romania and is a major addition to thecompanys expanding global footprint. In addition, Suzlon and Martifer also signed acontract for the supply of nine S88 turbines between the Baião and Vila Franca de Xirawind farms in Portugal for a total 18.9 MW of capacity.The orders are also the first supply of Suzlon wind turbines to Martifer of Portugal, amajor company within the energy and construction industry. The order for Romania isalready part of the consolidated order book position of the Company as declared on30th July 2008.Mr. Toine van Megen  CEO, Suzlon Energy Limited said: The order representsnot only the rapid globalization of our business, but also the value proposition Sulzonpresents to clients. We are very pleased to partner with Martifer; this represents animportant milestone in our European expansion.Mr. António Pontes, CEO - Martifer Energy Systems, said: "We are very happy tohave formalized the acquisition of the first Suzlon turbines for Martifer. We areconfident this is the first of many orders and that in the future we will be able toextend the special relationship we have with Suzlon to other markets."Mr. Erik Winther Pedersen  CEO, SWEAS said: These orders are the result of acommitted cooperation between client and manufacturer enabling for a unique scopeof delivery that are true to the spirit of delivering sustainable solutions.
