Press Release










December 28, 2015
 Suzlon organized an eye and dental screening camp for villagers in Kittampalayam, Coimbatore
113 villagers participated in the awareness and check-up camp15 villagers were referred to Sankara Eye hospital for cataract operationDistrict Blindness Eradication Society provided monetary help for the cataract operationsCoimbatore: Reiterating its commitment to improving the health and well-being of communities aroundits areas of operation, Suzlon Foundation, in association with IMAYAM, an NGO, Sankara Eye Hospitaland RVS Dental College and Hospital, organised an eye and dental screening camp at Kittampalayamvillage in Coimbatore. The objective of the camp was to create awareness in, and educate the localcommunities about dental and eye related diseases.A total of 113 villagers participated in the camp, out of which 15 villagers were diagnosed with cataractand referred to Sankara Eye Hospital where they will be provided treatment.The camp organized by Suzlon and its partners was attended by the village panchayat representativesincluding Mr.JothimaniRangasamy, President, Kittampalayam Panchayat and Mr.Venkatachalam, VicePresident, Kittampalayam Panchayat.Dr Jasmine Sofia Gogia, General Manager, Suzlon Foundation said, At Suzlon, we are guided by thephilosophy to pursue social, economic and ecological development for our planet. Our CSR initiativesfocus on inclusive growth and holistic development of the surrounding communities across our areas ofoperations. The awareness and screening program was organized to promote good health, preventdiseases, and facilitate early diagnosis and treatment. We thank all our partners for extending theirsupport to organize the awareness camp. Suzlon has wind energy installation of 728MW in Coimbatore spread across various locations. Over 298villages are covered under Suzlons CSR programs in Coimbatore.Sustainable development is at the core of Suzlons CSR outreach and we believe that we have theresponsibility to nurture and enhance the financial, natural, social, human and physical resourcesaround our operating areas. Suzlon Foundation, an autonomous body, is responsible for ensuring theintegration of CSR initiatives across all Suzlon business operations, and has developed a grassrootsframework which responds to the neighbourhood needs. Apart from being a technology leader, Suzlonaims to champion the cause of sustainable energy and create a lasting and harmonious environment.
