 In the video above, Sofar Ocean Data Scientist Galen Egan, PhD provides an in-depth explanation of Vessel Performance Models (VPM) A VPM calculates the relationship between a vessels propeller RPM, engine power, speed, and fuel consumption, and predicts how that relationship will change in different sea states . The VPM's predictions, in turn, inform the route guidance that a voyage optimization platform sends to a Captain .
