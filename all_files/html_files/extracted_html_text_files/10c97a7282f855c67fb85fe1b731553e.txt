January 20, 2011
Suzlon Wind Energy A/S appoints Alf Hansson as Head of Sales in Sweden
Aarhus, Denmark: Suzlon Wind Energy A/S, the European arm of SuzlonEnergy Limited, announced today the appointment of Mr. Alf Hansson as Head ofSales in Sweden.Mr. Frans HJ Visscher, CEO, Suzlon Wind Energy A/S, said: Sweden is animportant market for us with great potential. Mr. Hansson has vast experience inthe local market, especially in the energy sector. We are confident that with hisexpertise will be able to deliver exceptional solutions that are optimized for theSwedish market conditions and customer needs.Mr. Hanssons extensive local experience with energy in Sweden makes him aperfect fit for Suzlon. Prior to joining Suzlon he worked as business developer atStatkraft, one of the largest renewable energy companies on Europe and, beforethat, he worked in different positions for Vattenfall in Sweden. Mr. Hansson willbe based in Göteborg.Mr. Alf Hansson said: I am very pleased to join Suzlon as Head of Sales inSweden. I look forward to serving the Swedish market and am confident that ourcommitment to the market and our focus on customer needs will be of greatbenefit to Swedish customers.Sweden has more than 1,500 megawatt (MW) wind power already installed andan additional 3,000 MW is expected to be installed by 2015. Suzlon has a windpower project running in Assjö for Triventus.Notes to editorsIn 2009, the Swedish Government set forth an integrated climate and energypolicy for up to 2020. This forms the most ambitious climate and energy policyever presented by a European country and proposes highly ambitious targetsincluding that half of Swedens energy coming from renewable sources in 2020;Swedens net emissions of greenhouse gases being equal to zero by the middle ofthe century with a 40 per cent greenhouse gas emissions reduction target for2020.
