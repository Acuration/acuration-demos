July 8, 2009
Suzlon receives repeat order from KS Oils
    Repeat order for 10 units of S.821.5 MW turbine    Customer reposes full faith in SuzlonPune: Suzlon, Indias leading and worlds fifth largest wind power Solution Company gets arepeat order from Madhya Pradesh based KS Oils Limited.KS Oils started with a 2.5MW project in Madhya Pradesh with Suzlon and has placed 6 orders withSuzlon till date.Expanding our relationship with KS Oils demonstrates Suzlons ability to provide quality productsand services to the Indian market. We have provided exceptional customer service, which we believehas given us another opportunity to grow our highly-valued partnership with KS Oils. said Ishwar CMangal, President India Business of Suzlon.The repeat order from KS Oils makes us happy to be of service to a discerning customer such as KS Oilsand partner with them in growing their business said Mr. Sumant Sinha COO of SEL.Speaking on behalf of K.S Oils Limited, Mr. Sanjay Agarwal, MD said KS Oils is pleased to partnerwith Suzlon again as we persist to build momentum in our business. We are satisfied with ourexperience with them thus far and would like to do business with Indias leading wind powercompany. We look forward to building on this success as we develop the Rajasthan project.
