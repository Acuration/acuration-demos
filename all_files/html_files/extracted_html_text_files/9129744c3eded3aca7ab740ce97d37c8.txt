Suzlon Europe, Africa and LATAM
Suzlon Energy A/S has established itself as a major wind energy solutions provider in Europe within a short span of time with signed contracts totalling 773 MW (368 WTGs) for delivery to 29 sites in 9 countries across Europe.
Through its subsidiaries in Denmark, Spain, Portugal, Turkey, South Africa, Latin America and Romania, Suzlon Energy A/S has built an active business presence in the wind energy industry. Suzlon offers the complete gamut of activities in the wind energy value chain including commissioning, construction and development of a wind park through to maintenance of the wind turbines for their entire life cycle.
Suzlon enjoys immense brand equity among customers in Europe as a result of its ability to offer the complete range of technologically advanced, high performance, cost-effective and efficient wind energy solutions. It focuses on providing customers with the best-in-class service support to maximise the ROI and profitability of their wind energy projects.
Suzlon has operational facilities in Denmark and Madrid apart from multiple OMS teams spread across the continent for maintenance of its installed WTGs. These centres handle different aspects of the wind energy value chain ranging from IT support, SCADA development, W&S, spare parts storage and handling and developing blades.
