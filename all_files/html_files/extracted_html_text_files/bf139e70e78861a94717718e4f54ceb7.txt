Suzlon Europe, Africa and LATAM
Suzlon Energy A/S (SEAS) has established itself as a major wind energy solution provider in Europe for over more than one decade, with presence in 11 countries and +1.5 GW installed across Europe, Africa and LATAM.
Through Suzlon Energy A/S headquarter in Denmark and its subsidiaries in Spain, Portugal, Turkey, Romania, South Africa, and Nicaragua, Suzlon has built an active business presence in the European, African and LATAM wind energy markets. Suzlon Energy A/S is an OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) that stands behind its commitment to be there for the entire project lifecycle. Also, offers operational, maintenance and service contracts, as well as customized solutions and upgrades to ensure the continuous optimization of the existing fleet and improve the Return of Investment (ROI), availability and safety.
Suzlon is seen upon by our customers as a flexible partner who will always ensure to achieve the best result, high performance, cost-effective and efficient wind energy solutions. It focuses on providing customers with the best-in-class service to maximize the ROI and profitability of their wind energy projects.
