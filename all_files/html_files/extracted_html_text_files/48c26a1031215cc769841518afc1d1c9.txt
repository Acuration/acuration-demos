October 17, 2014
Suzlon group wins 150MW of orders from New & Existing customers
Suzlon has won turbine supply deals in India with a combined capacity of 150 MWSuzlon has bagged orders from SME sector across diverse industry verticals ; reiterates customer interest to invest in wind energy post reinstatement of the Accelerate DepreciationOrders predominantly comprise of its proven multi-megawatt product S97 2.1 MW wind turbine generator (WTG)Pune, India: Suzlon Group, the worlds fifth largest* wind turbine maker, today announced that it hasbeen awarded orders for a combined capacity of 150 MW. This combined capacity includes ordersfrom corporate houses such as Malpani Group, Rajasthan Gum Group, KRBL, Sterling Agro Group andan assortment of SMEs( Small and Medium Enterprise). The 150 MW order received is a significantdevelopment since it demonstrates renewed customer interest to harness wind energy post thereinstatement of Accelerated Depreciation (AD) policy by the Government of India. The projects arescheduled for execution across eight states in India with high wind potential.Suzlon will supply its robust multi-megawatt S97-90m WTGs and will leverage on its turnkey windsolutions expertise to oversee the project operations from start to end. The innovative S97- 90mproduct portfolio is designed to harness the optimal available wind resources and deliver higherenergy, productivity, improved serviceability which in turn ensures higher ROI to customers. Theprojects will be located across all the 8 wind states of India (Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, Tamil Nadu,Andhra Pradesh, Rajasthan, Karnataka and Kerala) as appended:The repeat orders from our existing customers - Malpani Group, Rajasthan Gum Group, KRBL Group,and Sterling Agro Group reinforces the confidence customers have in Suzlons innovative productsand service capabilities. The new project wins from customers in the SME sector reaffirms Suzlonscapabilities to offer customized solutions across regions and is a testament of their trust in Suzlonspioneering concept to commissioning approach.Speaking on the new order wins, Mr Tulsi Tanti, Chairman  Suzlon Group, said: Leveraging on ourleadership position within the country, we continue to have a strong focus on the Indian market as itoffers a favorable renewable energy environment especially now with Governments clear thrust onclean energy. These orders have added a fillip to our robust order book. We stand committed tobuild on our technological edge and offer new age products and best in class services. We areconfident that our business will add value to the energy security needs of our country. Our mission isto make profitable and efficient wind energy accessible within the country.Mr Rajesh Malpani, Managing Director, Malpani Group said, We have been working with SuzlonGroup for over 13 years and we are delighted to continue the relationship forward with the new29.4 MW project to be located in Madhya Pradesh. We are extremely satisfied with the performanceof Suzlons WTGs and their service delivery. The new contract is an extension of our faith and trust inSuzlons wind energy solutions.Mr Satyanarayan Dhoot, Rajasthan Gum Group, With the help of Suzlon, we have been able tosignificantly reduce our power costs and contribute towards environmental sustainability. Suzlonscutting edge WTG technology and reliable service delivery through its OMS division has won ourconfidence over 9 years and the new order of 14.70 MW is a testimony of our trust in Suzlonscapabilities.Mr. Anoop Gupta, Jt. Managing Director, KRBL Limited, said, we have partnered with Suzlon groupfor over 50 MW projects and have complete faith in their products and service capabilities. The newcontract of 23.10 MW signed with Suzlon demonstrates our confidence in the consistency of theirperformance.Mr. Sharad Saluja, Director, Sterling Agro Industries Ltd, said, Suzlons experience in execution ofEPC solutions is un-paralleled and hence they have been our partner of choice for around 4 years.Our investment in wind energy is a small step to reduce our carbon footprint and contribute towardsa greener tomorrow. We look forward to combining our synergies to develop the upcoming Windpower project in Maharashtra and Andhra Pradesh.Suzlons growth journey is aligned to Indias vision of energy security and our efforts are committedto bring down cost of energy and provide clean and affordable energy for all.
