March 13, 2009
Suzlon Appoints New Chief Financial Officer
Pune: Suzlon Energy Limited (SEL), the world's fifth leading wind turbine maker,named Mr. Robin Banerjee as its new Chief Financial Officer. He has joined thecompany with immediate effect.Mr. Banerjee comes to Suzlon with a rich international experience across a diverserange of businesses. He has held senior leadership positions with responsibilitiesacross global strategy, M&A, Finance, and Treasury amongst others, across variousindustries like FMCG, Steel, Auto Components and Travel.Commenting on the appointment, Mr. Sumant Sinha, COO - Suzlon Energy, said, Weare pleased to welcome Robin Banerjee to Suzlon Energys management team. Hisbroad financial experience and extensive expertise will not only bring tremendousvalue to the company but will also drive our prime focus on building value for ourshareholders.Added Mr. Tulsi Tanti, CMD  Suzlon Energy: The world today faces a challengingeconomic landscape. Robin Banerjee with his considerable experience in large,complex, multinational organizations is a valuable addition to the Suzlon managementteam.The office of the CFO was previously shared between Mr. Kirti Vagadia, Head Corporate Finance and Mr. Sumant Sinha. The Finance responsibilities for SEL will nowtransition to Mr. Banerjee as CFO, reporting to Mr. Sinha; while Mr. Vagadia will takeover Group-level Finance responsibilities.
