Press Release
December 29, 2015
Suzlon organized an awareness programme on Dengue prevention in Melailandakulam village, Tirunelveli
The awareness programme was attended by 2,000 villagers500 litres of Nilavembu Kudineer(medicine prepared from herbs)was distributed to 2,000 villagersTirunelveli: Suzlon Foundation, in partnership with the Association for Integrated Rural Development(AIRD) and representatives of Melailandakulam village, organised an awareness programme on theprevention of Dengue and other water borne diseases. The initiative was undertaken to educate thevillagers on the prevention of, and methods to cure, water borne diseases, especially with the recentheavy rains in Tamil Nadu.In addition to raising awareness, Nilavembu Kudineer, a potent anti-viral medicine against virusescausing Dengue and Chikungunya fevers, was distributed to 2,000 villagers who attended theprogramme. The medicine is recognized by the government health department as an effectivetreatment during epidemics.Speaking about the initiative, Dr. Jasmine Sofia Gogia, General Manager, Suzlon Foundation said,Given that dengue cases are increasing at an alarming rate, it is imperative to spread awareness on thecauses of dengue and the preventive measures. It was a collaborative effort with the support of doctorsfrom nearby hospitals, the village panchayat and active participation of the villagers. Suzlon endeavoursto achieve social, economic and ecological development around all its areas of operations.Sustainable development is at the core of Suzlons CSR outreach and we believe that we have theresponsibility to nurture and enhance the financial, natural, social, human and physical resourcesaround our operating areas. Suzlon Foundation, an autonomous body, is responsible for ensuring theintegration of CSR initiatives across all Suzlon business operations, and has developed a grassrootsframework which responds to the neighbourhood needs. Apart from being a technology leader, Suzlonaims to champion the cause of sustainable energy and create a lasting and harmonious environment.
