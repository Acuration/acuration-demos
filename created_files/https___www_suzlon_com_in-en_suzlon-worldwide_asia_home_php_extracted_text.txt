Asia


Asia

            Asia continues to be the cornerstone of Suzlon’s success in the field of renewable energy. Suzlon’s dominance in Asia, especially India, is underscored by the fact that it is number one amongst renewable energy companies in India. Suzlon has steadily expanded its footprint across Asia, with joint ventures in China and Sri Lanka. Today, it is a leading name in renewable energy across the continent. In addition to 14 manufacturing facilities in India. 
         
 
            Suzlon offers its entire range of solutions to customers in Asia - from commissioning and development of a wind farm, through to the life-time upkeep and maintenance. Suzlon also offers Engineering, Procurement and Construction services for wind farm projects. 
         








India



Suzlon is headquartered in Pune, India. Since its establishment in India in 1995, India has remained the hub of Suzlon’s manufacturing, research and development activities. With largest share of installed capacity, Suzlon is the leader of renewable energy in India.
Know More









China




Suzlon expanded its footprint in China by setting up a manufacturing facility in Tianjin Hi-tech industrial park in 2006. This was further enhanced with another joint venture in 2013. Suzlon has installed more than 929 MW of wind energy projects in China. 
Know More








sri lanka



SENOK Wind Power, a JV between Suzlon and Colombo-based conglomerate SENOK Trade Combine, has been harnessing wind energy in Sri Lanka since 2010.
