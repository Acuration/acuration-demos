What is
 
green hydrogen
?
Green hydrogen is produced by splitting water into hydrogen and oxygen using a renewable 
energy source, like solar or wind.
G
reen hydrogen
 
has various uses
 
in
 
reduc
ing
 
the impact of climate change
, including
 
decarbonizing 
sectors such as steel and cement production
 
and
 
for
 
clean
 
energy storage.
Why RES?
Our vision is to create a world where everyone has access to affordable zero carbon energy. 

Providing a source of green hydrogen from renewables that is insulated from present and future gas price volatility helps us limit environmental impact and support the decarbonization of industry.
