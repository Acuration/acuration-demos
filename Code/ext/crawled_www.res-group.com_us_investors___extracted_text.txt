Delivering high quality projects
With a development portfolio spanning multiple markets and across a range of different technologies, we’re able to support your investment needs with high performing assets.
Our collaborative approach to developing projects involves building strong relationships with all stakeholders right from the start.
We ensure the projects we develop bring benefits, whether that’s through job creation, an injection of new business into the local economy, or enhancing the local infrastructure.
Why RES?
Tailoring our approach to each market means we can provide flexible support to suit your specific needs.

Our development portfolio stretches across multiple geographies, and our range of technologies provide high-performing opportunities with long-term sustainable revenue.
Wide portfolio
Our range of technologies and wide portfolio provides access to multiple markets.
Return on investment
Securing long-term value and sustainable returns on your investment.
Community relationships
We value building long-term relationships with landowners, First Nations and local communities who host the projects we develop. 
Flexible independence
We’re independently owned, bringing flexibility to the way we work.
ESG conscious
Our commitment to ESG goes far beyond investment: it’s how we operate and run our business.
