Contact us Europe Americas Australia Denmark France Germany Ireland Norway Portugal Sweden Spain Türkiye UK USA Canada Australia Home About us Safety About us Safety Through harnessing our culture of care, we’re committed to achieving a world-class level of safety performance. Our approach Safety is of the utmost importance. We strive to ensure that everyone that works for us, and alongside us, goes home safe every day. Our ambition is to continue to lead the market in delivering best-in-class health and safety performance, as we simultaneously look to the future in developing a zero-harm culture. Safety focus events Getting everyone together to explore a number of safety topics. Internal culture surveys Helping to optimise the work of the HSQE team and internal safety campaigns. Team ownership Safety objectives are embedded into personal development and performance reviews. We work across all the different renewable energy technologies – wind, solar, battery storage and green hydrogen. All these technologies are experiencing significant change, and each have unique health and safety challenges. It is a priority for us to be proactive in championing safety in each of these technologies Michael Sinclair-Williams Global HSQE Director HSQE management systems A management system is integrated into every RES project – supported by checklists, inspection documents, and templates. Training and development Online training, certified internal trainers, and curriculums tailored to roles and responsibilities. Our commitment Safety leadership and processes are overseen at the highest level, supported by the global Safety Leadership Team (SLT). We continually measure our safety performance to assess how we’re performing, advocating the reporting of ‘good catches’ and ‘near misses’ to help identify issues and prevent incidents and accidents. Taking time to host quarterly safety focus events reinforces our safety commitment and provides our teams with the knowledge and understanding to do their jobs safely.
