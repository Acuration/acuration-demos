The SHE Awards celebrate innovation and achievements in health and safety and winning is deserved recognition of RES’ dedication to safety in all areas of the business. Hugh Dennis, the well-known actor and comedian, kept his humour carefully safe for the occasion, and presented the award to the RES team of Simon Deacon, Ian McCallum CMIOSH., Jessica Ferguson, Slav Litwin, Anne Anderson and Lynsey Stewart. The win in the Team category is especially prestigious as the Team award is sponsored by  NEBOSH , the UK-based National Examination Board in Occupational Safety and Health. Michael Sinclair-Williams, Group HSQE Director was delighted. “Winning the Team SHE Award is a fantastic achievement for RES. Safety is one of our core strategic objectives and achieving market leading safety performance is something everyone at RES works towards. Winning is confirmation of the great strides we are making towards this goal”. Over the past few years the HSQE team have supported the leadership and wider business, to reduce our incident frequency rate. On 12 th May we will be holding another company wide ‘Don’t Risk It’ Safety Focus Event where two and half thousand people globally will stand down for the day to take part in a day of activities to enhance RES safety culture. About RES RES is the world’s largest independent renewable energy company active in onshore and offshore wind, solar, energy storage and transmission and distribution. At the forefront of the industry for over 40 years, RES has delivered more than 22GW of renewable energy projects across the globe and supports an operational asset portfolio exceeding 9GW worldwide for a large client base. Understanding the unique needs of corporate clients, RES has secured over 1.5GW of corporate power purchase agreements (PPAs) enabling access to energy at the lowest cost.
