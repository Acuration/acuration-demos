What is transmission & distribution ? Transmission & distribution networks move electricity to the grid and then on to end consumers. This infrastructure is crucial: it powers local homes and businesses, ensures energy is continually available, and creates a stable, reliable network. Why RES? Our broad range of solutions deliver energy when and where it’s needed for a safer, more reliable grid. We install, maintain, and restore projects in locations all over the US. We’re committed to reducing grid congestion and in turn, cutting down on risk and improving safe and efficient distribution of energy.
