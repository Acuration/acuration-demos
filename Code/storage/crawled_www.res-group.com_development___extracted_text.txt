Collaboration at our core
We develop successful clean energy projects around the world. We ensure our projects generate maximum value, and build meaningful relationships with landowners, local communities, First Nations, investors, governments, and authorities along the way.
Our engagement ethos is central to how we develop our projects – informing our approach and helping us drive positive benefits for all.
We always maintain constant communication with everyone involved, working closely together to ensure all parties know what’s happening and when.
What we offer
We have decades of experience in developing successful clean energy projects.
Multiple technologies
Expertise across onshore wind, offshore wind, solar PV, energy storage, and green hydrogen.
Wide-ranging benefits
Landowners and local communities all benefit from development projects.
Positive impact
Sustainability powers everything we do and the decisions we make.
Global reach
We’ve developed over 23GW of clean energy projects worldwide.

Why RES?
Together, we can realise the long-term economic and environmental benefits that delivering a clean energy future will bring.

Our highly skilled people use cutting-edge tools created in house, plus state-of-the-art meteorological and mapping studies to identify potential sites with the best natural resource and suitable grid connections.
