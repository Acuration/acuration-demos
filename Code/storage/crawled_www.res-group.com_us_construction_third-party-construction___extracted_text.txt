Construction projects, start to 
finish
 
We build clean energy assets on behalf of investors and owners across a range of global markets.
With construction expertise in solar, onshore and offshore wind, storage and hydrogen, our customers rely on us for safe and efficient project delivery from start to finish.
Why RES? 
Providing you with the right team to achieve safe and timely construction projects.  You’ll have access to our people - seasoned delivery experts and capable problem-solvers, who will work collaboratively with you to complete successful builds.

We can support you navigate permitting, optimize design, self-perform construction and interconnection, and manage commissioning – all to your schedule and budget.  
Health and safety 
People are our passion. Everyone going home safe everyday governs our approach to design and guides every action we take in the field. 
Reliable delivery
Extensive engineering and construction experience across multiple successful projects means we can clearly demonstrate a track record of reliability 
Quality
Delivering a projects that operate the way you and your lenders expect them to, year after year.  We takes a long-term view that underpins our commitment to quality ensuring the ultimate return on investment for our customers.  
One contract, seamless execution 
By coordinating and self-performing every stage, we end to end project execution, saving you time, hassle and spend.  
What we offer
Trust and reliability are key to successful project delivery. Our experience in construction and engineering across a range of clean energy technologies means you’re in safe hands.
