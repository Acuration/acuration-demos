The growth in this segment is already huge, as are the opportunities for UK companies to grow in this market. RenewableUK’s & ORE Catapult’s O&M Conference is about delivering on these opportunities while overcoming challenges. Focussed on feedback and lessons learned from companies and people that are delivering practical growth – making things happen and willing to share their experiences. As headline sponsor of the event, our O&M team will be attending and be on hand to answer any of your questions. We look forward to seeing you there.
