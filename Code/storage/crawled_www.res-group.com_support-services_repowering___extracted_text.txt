What is
 
repowering
?
Repowering is the
 
renewal of existing
 
clean energy
 
projects
, where
 
parts of
 
a plant
 
are 
replaced and technically optimised.
The aim of repowering is to extend a
 
renewable
 
asset’s lifespan, improve efficiency, and produce a 
larger amount of energy than before.
