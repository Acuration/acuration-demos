April 25, 2014
 Suzlon Group wins 370 MW of orders globally in the Q4 OF FY 13-14
Pune/Hamburg: Suzlon Group, the worlds fifth largest wind turbine manufacturer has signedcontracts for 370 MW during the Q4 of FY 2013-14, adding to an already strong pipeline ofonshore & off shore orders globally in 2014. The company will be supplying these turbines fornew wind farms in countries such as Germany, UK, India, Belgium and other Europeancountries.Suzlon Group over the years has an installed capacity of 24200 MW globally across 33countries. With an impressive product portfolios and best in class service, our order Suzlonorder books stands at USD $ 7.7 billion. Suzlon Group has successfully retained its 5th placeamong global wind turbine makers in 2013, according to MAKE ConsultingAndreas Nauen, CEO  Senvion SE, commented: We have a suitable turbine for each individualsite in our product portfolio. Thats why we are optimistic to extend our market share inSenvions key markets.Mr Kirti Vagadia Group Head  Corporate Finance. Suzlon Group said: The orders representnot only the rapid globalization of our business, but also the value proposition Suzlon has tooffer to its clients..In its annual Global Wind Turbine OEM Market Share report, MAKE said Suzlon had a globalmarket share of 6.3%, as measured by newly installed generating capacity.Under the strong leadership of Mr.Tulsi Tanti and his astute ability to gauge the tremendouspotential and advantages of the industry, Suzlon continues to be a formidable Global WindEnergy player today.
