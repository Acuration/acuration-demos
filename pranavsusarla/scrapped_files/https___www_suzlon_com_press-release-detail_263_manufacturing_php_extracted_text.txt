July 8, 2009
 Suzlon receives another order from Datang China
 Repeat order increases total supply to Datang to 133.75 MWRepeat orders from existing customers are a sure sign of their confidence in Suzlon  Sumant Sinha COO, Suzlon China: Suzlon Energy (Tianjin) Limited a subsidiary of Suzlon Energy Limited (SEL), Indias leadingand worlds fifth largest wind power solutions company; strengthened its presence in the Chinesewind energy market with another order for 48.75 MW. The contract, for 39 sets of S.64-1.25 MWcapacity was signed with Datang Power Generation Co.Datang Power Generation Co. is one of top five power producers in China. The previous order fromthe same customer was received for Zhuozi Phase I Project in Inner Mongolia and Liu'ao Project inFujian Province.Speaking about the order, Mr. Paulo Soares  CEO Suzlon Energy Tianjin Ltd said:We are very happy with this repeat order from Datang. These turbines will be supplied asextension of Zhuozi Wind Farm Phase I. Suzlon has previously supplied a total of 68 units of the S64-1.25MW to this key customer for the same wind farm and for another project in Liuao,Zhangzhou, Fujian Province. With this order, our total supply portfolio to Datang Power will beincreased to 133.75MW of which 68 turbines of 85MW capacity are already operational.Mr. Sumant Sinha, COO, SEL, said on the occasion, For us in Suzlon, repeat orders from existingcustomers are a sure sign of their confidence in us, our product and the overall experience that theyhave with us. We view this order from Datang as a testimony to the way our turbines haveperformed in their earlier projects, and therefore will strive to ensure that it continues in this projectas wellThe turbines under this order are expected to be delivered and commissioned by the third quarterof FY 2009-10.
