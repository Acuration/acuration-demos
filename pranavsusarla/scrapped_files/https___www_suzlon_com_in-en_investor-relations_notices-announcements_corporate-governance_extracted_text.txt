Corporate Governance Policies



                        Suzlon believes corporate governance is more about values and ethics than about rules and policies. It values its relationships with its stakeholders and strives to maintain their
                        trust at the highest level at all times. 

                        Suzlon has a set of policies and programmes to guide its people on how to conduct business and observe the tenets of good governance in their day-to-day functioning.
                    


Here are some of Suzlon’s key policies and programmes:




Terms and conditions for appointment of Independent Directors




Code of Practices and Procedures for Fair Disclosure of Unpublished Price Sensitive Information




Code of Conduct to regulate, monitor and report trading by insiders (the "Insider Trading Code")




Whistle Blower Policy / Vigil Mechanism




Corporate governance and code of ethics
