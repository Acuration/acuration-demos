July 1, 2015
 Suzlon achieves a milestone by installing its 10,000th Wind Turbine Generator
Global fleet producing ~ 32 TWh per annum of wind energy, equivalent to powering approximately 9 million households annuallyDemonstrates Suzlons evolving technological and manufacturing prowess and project execution capabilitiesPune, India: Suzlon Group, one of the leading wind turbine manufacturer, announced a significantmilestone achievement of installing its 10,000th WTG (wind turbine generator) during the first half of2015. Suzlon has come a long way since its maiden turbine installation in the state of Gujarat, Indiain 1995.With over two decades of operating history, Suzlon Group has cumulative installations of over 14 GWof wind energy capacity with operations across over 17 countries and customer base of more than1700.Mr. Tulsi Tanti, Chairman, Suzlon Group said , Suzlons operations commenced in 1995 with just ahandful of people and along the way built a team that made it possible to achieve the installation ofthis commendable feat. This milestone reinforces the confidence our customers have on Suzlon andalso on its products and project execution capabilities. I would like to take this opportunity to thankall our customers for their continuous belief on us and our employees for their relentless support.We will continuously work at reducing the cost of energy by about 15 per cent by 2020 and heavilyinvest in R&D and innovation. I am confident that wind energy will play a pivotal role in changing theglobal energy landscape and Suzlon is best positioned to tap this growing opportunity for a greenertomorrow.Suzlon continues to leverage its technological edge to introduce path breaking products aimed athigher yields and ensuring higher ROI to customers. The products like S97-120m hub height hybridtower and the soon to be launched S111-2.1MW Turbine, the largest rotor diameter WTG areindustry game changers, engineered for low wind sites.Suzlons state of the art product offerings and best in class service are developed to harness theoptimal available wind resources across diverse geographies that deliver higher energy, productivityand improved serviceability.Suzlon's end to end solutions approach as a component manufacturer, wind turbine manufacturer,project development & execution capabilities and dedicated Life Cycle Asset Management servicesgives it the competitive edge.
